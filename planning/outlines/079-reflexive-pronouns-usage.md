## Research / Grounding Notes

- **Core concept:** Reflexive pronouns refer back to the subject, add emphasis, or appear in a few fixed expressions.
- **Key rule:** Use myself/yourself/etc. only when the subject and object are the same or for emphasis.
- **References:** Quirk et al., A Comprehensive Grammar of the English Language; Huddleston & Pullum, The Cambridge Grammar of the English Language; Swan, Practical English Usage; Murphy, English Grammar in Use.
- **Common errors:** Myself Keshav. → I am Keshav.; Rahul and myself went. → Rahul and I went.; I hurt me. → I hurt myself.; She herself completed it. (emphasis correct).
