## परिचय: वो Pronoun जो धीरे-धीरे गायब हो रहा है

"Whom" everyday spoken English से धीरे-धीरे गायब हो रहा है। Casual conversation में लगभग सब "who" कहते हैं। लेकिन परीक्षाएँ अभी भी इस फरक को test करती हैं।

अच्छी बात: सही चुनने का trick आपके सोचने से भी आसान है।

---

## He/Him Trick

"who" और "whom" चुनने का सबसे आसान तरीका:

1. मन में "he" या "him" से जवाब देखें।
2. अगर "he" काम करता है → **who** (subject)।
3. अगर "him" काम करता है → **whom** (object)।

(नोटिस: h**e** = wh**o**; h**i**m = wh**o**m. Same letters.)

### उदाहरण

> **Who/Whom** is coming? → **He** is coming. → **Who** is coming.

> **Who/Whom** did you invite? → I invited **him**. → **Whom** did you invite.

> The man **who/whom** I met was kind. → I met **him**. → The man **whom** I met.

> The man **who/whom** called was angry. → **He** called. → The man **who** called.

---

## "Who" — Subject

"Who" action perform करता है। यह clause का subject है।

> **Who** wrote this letter? (किसने लिखा?)

> The person **who** called yesterday is my boss.

> I don't know **who** is responsible.

---

## "Whom" — Object

"Whom" action receive करता है। यह verb या preposition का object है।

> **Whom** did you see? (object of "see")

> The woman **whom** I interviewed was impressive.

> To **whom** should I address this letter? (preposition "to" का object)

> With **whom** did you go?

---

## जब "Whom" Stiff लगता है

Informal English में objects के लिए भी "who" इस्तेमाल होता है:

> "Who did you give it to?" (अनौपचारिक)

> "Who did you see?" (अनौपचारिक)

Formal writing और परीक्षाओं में objects के लिए "whom" expected है।

---

## आम गलतियाँ

| गलत | सही |
|---|---|
| Whom is at the door? | Who is at the door? |
| Who did you send it to? | Whom did you send it to? (औपचारिक) |
| The person whom is responsible | The person who is responsible |

---

## अभ्यास प्रश्न

1. ______ is your favourite teacher?
2. ______ did you vote for?
3. The candidate ______ won was unexpected.
4. To ______ did you speak?
5. She is the person ______ I was talking about.

### उत्तर कुंजी

1. **Who** — subject ("he is your favourite teacher")
2. **Whom** — object ("I voted for him")
3. **who** — subject ("he won")
4. **whom** — preposition "to" का object
5. **whom** — object ("I was talking about him")

---

## सारांश

- **Who** = subject (he). Action perform करता है।
- **Whom** = object (him). Action receive करता है।
- Substitution trick: "he" काम करे → who; "him" काम करे → whom।
- परीक्षाओं में objects के लिए "whom" इस्तेमाल करें।
