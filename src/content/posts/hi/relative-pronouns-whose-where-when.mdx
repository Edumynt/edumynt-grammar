## परिचय: "The Man Which Car" ग़लत है

Relative pronouns clauses को nouns से जोड़ते हैं। अधिकांश छात्र *who, which, that* जानते हैं — लेकिन *whose, where, when,* और *whereby* कन्फ़्यूज़ करते हैं।

> ❌ The man **which** car was stolen reported it to the police.  
> ✅ The man **whose** car was stolen reported it to the police.

यह article समझाता है कि clause के अंदर की role के आधार पर सही relative word कैसे चुनें।

---

## मूल अर्थ और नियम

> **Rule box:** Relative word **clause के अंदर की role** के आधार पर चुनें, सिर्फ़ पहले आने वाले noun के आधार पर नहीं। **Whose** = possession। **Where** = place। **When** = time। **Whereby** = by which / through which (formal)।

---

## रूप, पैटर्न और वाक्य में स्थिति

### 1. Whose (Possession)

*Whose* possession दिखाता है। यह लोगों, जानवरों और चीज़ों पर लागू हो सकता है।

> The man **whose** car was stolen reported it.  
> The book **whose** cover is red is mine.  
> The company **whose** CEO resigned is struggling.

**आम ग़लती:** Possession के लिए *which* उपयोग करना।

> ❌ The man **which** car was stolen...  
> ✅ The man **whose** car was stolen...

### 2. Where (Place)

*Place* को refer करता है।

> This is the house **where** I grew up.  
> The restaurant **where** we met is now closed.  
> The place **where** I was born is a small village.

### 3. When (Time)

*Time* को refer करता है।

> I remember the day **when** we first met.  
> Summer is the season **when** most people travel.  
> The moment **when** she walked in, everyone stared.

**आम ग़लती:** Time के लिए *where* उपयोग करना।

> ❌ The day **where** we met...  
> ✅ The day **when** we met...

### 4. Whereby (Formal: By Which)

*Whereby* एक formal relative pronoun है जिसका अर्थ है *by which* या *through which*।

> This is a system **whereby** users can apply online.  
> We need a process **whereby** complaints can be addressed.

*Whereby* मुख्यः formal, legal और academic writing में उपयोग होता है।

### 5. Quick Reference Table

| Relative Word | Refers To | Role in Clause |
|---|---|---|
| **who** | people | subject |
| **which** | things | subject/object |
| **whose** | people/things | possession |
| **where** | places | adverb of place |
| **when** | times | adverb of time |
| **whereby** | systems/methods | by which (formal) |

---

## Decision Checklist

1. **क्या clause को possessive चाहिए?** → **Whose** उपयोग करें।
2. **क्या clause place refer करता है?** → **Where** उपयोग करें।
3. **क्या clause time refer करता है?** → **When** उपयोग करें।
4. **क्या clause का अर्थ "by which" है (formal)?** → **Whereby** उपयोग करें।

## अंतर दिखाने वाले उदाहरण

1. The man **whose** car was stolen reported it. *(possession)*
2. This is the house **where** I grew up. *(place)*
3. I remember the day **when** we met. *(time)*
4. A system **whereby** users can apply online. *(formal)*
5. The book **whose** cover is torn is mine. *(possession — thing)*
6. The restaurant **where** we dined is closed. *(place)*
7. The moment **when** she arrived was perfect. *(time)*
8. She is a teacher **whose** students love her. *(possession)*
9. The city **where** I was born is beautiful. *(place)*
10. The process **whereby** decisions are made is transparent. *(formal)*

---

## Exam Traps और Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Things के लिए Which vs Whose

> ❌ The book **which** cover is torn...  
> ✅ The book **whose** cover is torn...

*Whose* चीज़ों पर भी लागू होता है।

### Trap 2: Time के लिए Where

> ❌ The day **where** we met...  
> ✅ The day **when** we met...

Time के लिए *when*, place के लिए *where*।

### Trap 3: Whereby Formal है

> ✅ This is a system **whereby** users can apply. *(formal)*  
> ✅ This is a system **that** users can apply **through**. *(less formal)*

## आम गलतियाँ

| ग़लत | सही | क्यों |
|---|---|---|
| The man which car... | The man **whose** car... | Possession के लिए whose। |
| The place which I was born. | The place **where** I was born. | Place के लिए where। |
| The day where we met. | The day **when** we met. | Time के लिए when। |

## Quick Practice

सही relative pronoun चुनिए।

1. The man ___ car was stolen reported it. (which / whose)
2. This is the house ___ I grew up. (where / which)
3. I remember the day ___ we met. (when / where)
4. Error spotting: The book which cover is torn.
5. Error spotting: The day where we first met.
6. Fill in the blank: The restaurant ___ we dined is closed. (where / which)
7. Rewrite correctly: The man which house was damaged.
8. Choose: The moment ___ she arrived was perfect. (when / where)

### Answer Key

1. **whose** — possession।  
2. **where** — place।  
3. **when** — time।  
4. **The book whose cover is torn.**  
5. **The day when we first met.**  
6. **where** — place।  
7. **The man whose house was damaged.**  
8. **when** — time।
