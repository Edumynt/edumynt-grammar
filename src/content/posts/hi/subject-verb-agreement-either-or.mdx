## Introduction: "Either Ram or His Friends Is" गलत क्यों है?

**Either/or** और **neither/nor** agreement problems बनाते हैं क्योंकि ये दो possible subjects को connect करते हैं, और दोनों subjects का number अलग हो सकता है।

> गलत: Either Ram or his friends **is** coming.  
> सही: Either Ram or his friends **are** coming.

अब order reverse करें:

> सही: Either his friends or Ram **is** coming.

Meaning similar है, लेकिन verb बदल गया क्योंकि verb के nearest subject बदल गया। इसी वजह से यह topic error spotting और sentence correction में बहुत common है।

Same pattern **neither/nor** के साथ भी आता है:

> गलत: Neither the students nor the teacher **are** ready.  
> सही: Neither the students nor the teacher **is** ready.

Verb nearer subject **teacher** से agree करता है।

Related agreement traps के लिए [Subject-Verb Agreement with "As Well As"](/hi/posts/subject-verb-agreement-as-well-as/) और [Subject-Verb Agreement with "One of"](/hi/posts/subject-verb-agreement-one-of/) देखें।

## Core Meaning and Rule

**Either...or** alternatives connect करता है। Meaning है: one option or the other.

> Either the manager or the assistants will call you.

**Neither...nor** दो negative alternatives connect करता है। Meaning है: not this and not that.

> Neither the manager nor the assistants have called.

जब two subjects **either/or** या **neither/nor** से joined हों, तो verb often nearest subject से agree करता है, especially जब subjects number या person में different हों।

> **Rule box:** **Either/or** और **neither/nor** के साथ verb को closest subject से agree कराएं। Structure parallel भी रखें।

Examples:

> Either Ram or his friends **are** coming.  
> Either his friends or Ram **is** coming.  
> Neither the students nor the teacher **is** ready.  
> Neither the teacher nor the students **are** ready.

इसे अक्सर **proximity rule** या **nearest-subject rule** कहा जाता है।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### Basic Agreement Patterns

| Structure | Nearer Subject | Correct Verb | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| either singular or singular | singular | singular | Either Ram or Shyam **is** responsible. |
| either singular or plural | plural | plural | Either Ram or his friends **are** coming. |
| either plural or singular | singular | singular | Either his friends or Ram **is** coming. |
| neither plural nor singular | singular | singular | Neither the students nor the teacher **is** ready. |
| neither singular nor plural | plural | plural | Neither the teacher nor the students **are** ready. |

जब दोनों subjects singular हों, verb singular होगा:

> Either the pen or the pencil **is** missing.  
> Neither Riya nor Meera **has** replied.

जब दोनों subjects plural हों, verb plural होगा:

> Either the boys or the girls **are** responsible.  
> Neither the teachers nor the students **were** informed.

Real difficulty तब आती है जब one subject singular हो और दूसरा plural।

### Person Agreement: You, I, He, They

Number ही issue नहीं है। Person भी matter करता है।

> Either you or I **am** wrong.  
> Either I or you **are** wrong.  
> Neither he nor she **has** answered.  
> Neither they nor he **has** accepted the offer.

Verb nearest subject से agree करता है:

- **I am**
- **you are**
- **he/she/it is/has/does**
- **they are/have/do**

लेकिन **Either you or I am wrong** जैसे sentences grammatically defensible होते हुए भी awkward लग सकते हैं। Natural writing में rewrite बेहतर है:

> One of us is wrong.  
> Either you are wrong, or I am.

### Parallel Structure

Correlative conjunctions में दोनों parts grammatically match करने चाहिए:

> Better: Either **the manager** or **the assistant** will call.  
> Weak: Either **the manager** or **calling the assistant** will solve it.

Same rule **neither/nor** के साथ:

> Better: Neither **the teacher** nor **the students** were informed.  
> Weak: Neither **the teacher** nor **informing the students** helped.

Parallelism sentence को easier and cleaner बनाता है।

## Decision Checklist

Exam questions solve करते समय यह method use करें:

1. Pair find करें: **either...or** या **neither...nor**.
2. दोनों joined subjects identify करें.
3. Verb के closest subject को देखें.
4. उसी nearest subject के according verb choose करें.
5. Person agreement check करें: **I am**, **you are**, **he/she has**, **they have**.
6. देखें कि sentence rewrite करने पर clearer होगा या नहीं.

### Checklist Example 1

> Either Ram or his friends is/are coming.

Nearer subject **friends** plural है। Correct:

> Either Ram or his friends **are** coming.

### Checklist Example 2

> Neither the students nor the teacher is/are ready.

Nearer subject **teacher** singular है। Correct:

> Neither the students nor the teacher **is** ready.

### Checklist Example 3

> Either you or I is/am/are wrong.

Nearer subject **I** है। Correct:

> Either you or I **am** wrong.

Smoother rewrite:

> One of us is wrong.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. **Either Ram or his friends are** coming.  
   Nearer subject **friends** plural है।

2. **Either his friends or Ram is** coming.  
   Nearer subject **Ram** singular है।

3. **Neither the students nor the teacher is** ready.  
   Nearer subject **teacher** singular है।

4. **Neither the teacher nor the students are** ready.  
   Nearer subject **students** plural है।

5. **Either the manager or the assistants have** the files.  
   **Assistants** verb के nearest है।

6. **Either the assistants or the manager has** the files.  
   **Manager** verb के nearest है।

7. **Either you or I am** wrong.  
   Verb **I** से agree करता है।

8. **Either I or you are** responsible.  
   Verb **you** से agree करता है।

9. **Neither he nor she has** answered.  
   **She** singular है, इसलिए **has**।

10. **Neither she nor they have** answered.  
   **They** plural है, इसलिए **have**।

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Verb को First Subject से Agree कराना

> गलत: Either Ram or his friends **is** coming.  
> सही: Either Ram or his friends **are** coming.

First subject से automatically agree न करें। **Either/or** और **neither/nor** में nearest subject usually verb control करता है।

### Trap 2: Subject Order Ignore करना

> Neither the students nor the teacher **is** ready.  
> Neither the teacher nor the students **are** ready.

दोनों sentences same people को refer कर सकते हैं, लेकिन order बदलने से verb बदल जाता है।

यह [Subject-Verb Agreement with "As Well As"](/hi/posts/subject-verb-agreement-as-well-as/) से related है, जहाँ word order correct verb को affect करता है।

### Trap 3: "Either You or I Are"

> Formal agreement में गलत: Either you or I **are** wrong.  
> सही: Either you or I **am** wrong.

Nearer subject **I** है, इसलिए formal grammar में **am** चाहिए। लेकिन यह stiff लग सकता है। Good writing में rewrite करें:

> Either you are wrong, or I am.  
> One of us is wrong.

### Trap 4: "Neither He Nor She Have"

> गलत: Neither he nor she **have** answered.  
> सही: Neither he nor she **has** answered.

Nearer subject **she** है, इसलिए **has**। **Neither** का negative meaning verb को plural नहीं बनाता।

### Trap 5: Parallelism Break करना

> Weak: Either the students must submit the form or the teacher.  
> Better: Either the students or the teacher must submit the form.

**Either/or** और **neither/nor** के दोनों sides grammatically balanced होने चाहिए। अगर एक side noun phrase है, दूसरी side भी noun phrase होनी चाहिए।

### Formal और Informal Usage

Conversation में speakers अक्सर natural-sounding verb choose करते हैं, especially mixed-person subjects में। Exams और formal writing में nearest-subject rule use करें। अगर result awkward लगे, sentence rewrite कर दें।

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Correct Form | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Either Ram or his friends **is** coming. | Either Ram or his friends **are** coming. | **Friends** nearer और plural है. |
| Neither the students nor the teacher **are** ready. | Neither the students nor the teacher **is** ready. | **Teacher** nearer और singular है. |
| Either you or I **are** wrong. | Either you or I **am** wrong. | **I** के साथ **am**. |
| Neither he nor she **have** answered. | Neither he nor she **has** answered. | **She** के साथ **has**. |
| Either the books or the file **are** missing. | Either the books or the file **is** missing. | **File** nearer और singular है. |
| Neither the manager nor the workers **has** agreed. | Neither the manager nor the workers **have** agreed. | **Workers** nearer और plural है. |

## Quick Practice

Correct option choose करें या error correct करें।

1. Either Ram or his friends is/are coming.
2. Neither the students nor the teacher is/are ready.
3. Either you or I am/are wrong.
4. Neither he nor she has/have answered.
5. Error spotting: Either the books or the file are missing.
6. Error spotting: Neither the manager nor the workers has agreed.
7. Error spotting: Either the principal or the teachers is responsible.
8. Fill in the blank: Neither the players nor the captain ___ available. *(is/are)*
9. Rewrite correctly: Either my parents or my brother have the key.
10. Rewrite more naturally: Either you or I am responsible.

### Answer Key

1. **are** — **friends** nearer और plural है.
2. **is** — **teacher** nearer और singular है.
3. **am** — **I** के साथ **am**.
4. **has** — **she** के साथ **has**.
5. Either the books or the file **is** missing.
6. Neither the manager nor the workers **have** agreed.
7. Either the principal or the teachers **are** responsible.
8. **is** — **captain** nearer और singular है.
9. Either my parents or my brother **has** the key.
10. Either you are responsible, or I am.

## Summary

**Either/or** और **neither/nor** alternatives connect करते हैं; ये ordinary **and** की तरह simple compound subject नहीं बनाते।

> **Rule box:** **Either/or** और **neither/nor** के साथ verb usually closest subject से agree करता है।

Memory trick:

> Verb से just पहले जो subject है, वही usually verb decide करता है।

Final examples:

> **Either Ram or his friends are** coming.  
> **Neither the students nor the teacher is** ready.  
> **Either you or I am** wrong — लेकिन **One of us is wrong** ज्यादा smooth है।
