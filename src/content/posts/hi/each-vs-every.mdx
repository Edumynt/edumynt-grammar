## परिचय: Meaning Plural, Grammar Singular

Learners अक्सर meaning में "many students" देखते हैं और plural verb चुन लेते हैं:

> गलत: Each students **are** ready.  
> सही: Each student **is** ready.

Sentence कई students के बारे में है, लेकिन **each** उन्हें one by one देखता है। इसलिए noun और verb singular होते हैं। **Every** भी similar way में काम करता है:

> Every student **is** ready.

"Each" और "every" का difference mainly focus का है। **Each** individual members पर focus करता है। **Every** पूरे group पर focus करता है। लेकिन दोनों normally singular noun और singular verb लेते हैं।

---

## Core Meaning और Rule

- **Each** = हर member को अलग-अलग consider करना, one by one.
- **Every** = group के सभी members को together consider करना.
- दोनों usually singular countable noun और singular verb लेते हैं।

> **Rule box:** **Each/every + singular noun + singular verb** use करें।

Compare करें:

> **Each student is** responsible for one chapter.  
> **Every student is** expected to attend.

पहले sentence में individual responsibility पर focus है। दूसरे में whole group requirement पर focus है।

---

## Forms, Patterns और Sentence Position

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| each/every + singular noun + singular verb | **Each book has** a code. / **Every room has** a fan. |
| each of + plural pronoun/noun + singular verb | **Each of them is** ready. |
| every one of + plural pronoun/noun + singular verb | **Every one of the players is** fit. |
| pronoun/noun + each | The students **each** received a certificate. |
| every + number/time expression | The bus comes **every ten minutes**. |

### Important Structure Difference

"Each" अकेला भी use हो सकता है और "of" के साथ भी:

> **Each** has a different role.  
> **Each of** the boys has a different role.

"Every" usually same way में alone use नहीं होता। Need हो तो **everyone**, **everybody**, या **every one** use करें।

> गलत: ~~Every of them came.~~  
> सही: **Every one of** them came.  
> सही: **Everyone** came.

## Decision Checklist

यह method use करें:

1. Word directly countable noun से पहले है? **Singular noun** use करें: each student, every book.
2. यह sentence का subject है? **Singular verb** use करें: is, has, does.
3. "Of" चाहिए? **Each of** use करें, "every of" नहीं।
4. Individual members पर focus है? **Each** prefer करें।
5. Whole group या regular repeated pattern पर focus है? **Every** prefer करें।

### Checklist Examples

> ___ student has a different roll number.

Focus individual है। Correct: **Each** student has a different roll number.

> ___ student must submit the form by Monday.

यह whole group के लिए rule है। Correct: **Every** student must submit the form by Monday.

> ___ of the answers is correct.

"Of" के साथ each use करें। Correct: **Each of** the answers is correct.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. **Each student is** ready.  
   Individual focus; one student at a time.

2. **Every student is** ready.  
   Whole group focus; कोई भी बाहर नहीं।

3. **Each of** the rooms has a window.  
   "Each of" + plural noun/pronoun, लेकिन singular verb.

4. **Every one of** them came on time.  
   "Every of them" नहीं।

5. The teacher gave **each student** a book.  
   One book to one student individually.

6. The teacher checked **every answer** carefully.  
   सभी answers check हुए।

7. The buses leave **every hour**.  
   Repeated time pattern.

8. The twins **each have** a bicycle.  
   "Each" plural subject के बाद है, इसलिए verb subject "twins" से agree करता है।

9. **Every child deserves** respect.  
   Singular noun और singular verb.

10. **Each side of** the argument has a point.  
    Sides को separately consider किया गया है।

## Singular Agreement Rule

सबसे ज्यादा test होने वाला rule agreement है:

> Each/every + singular noun + singular verb

| गलत | सही |
|---|---|
| Each students are ready. | Each student is ready. |
| Every rooms have a fan. | Every room has a fan. |
| Each of the boys are here. | Each of the boys is here. |
| Every one of the answers are correct. | Every one of the answers is correct. |

Meaning में plural idea verb control नहीं करता। Grammatical subject singular है।

## Each vs Every: Meaning Difference

### Individual Attention के लिए Each

जब separate members पर attention देना हो, **each** use करें।

> Each candidate will receive a separate email.  
> Each painting has its own style.

Meaning "one by one" के close है।

### Whole Group के लिए Every

जब all members without exception कहना हो, **every** use करें।

> Every citizen has the right to vote.  
> Every seat was full.

Meaning "all" के close है।

### Frequency के लिए Every

Repeated time या distance के लिए **every** normal है।

> every day  
> every week  
> every five kilometres

"Each day" possible है जब separate days emphasize करने हों, लेकिन routine के लिए "every day" ordinary expression है।

## Exam Traps और Usage Notes

### 1. Every of Wrong है

"Every of them" न लिखें।

> गलत: Every of them came.  
> सही: **Every one of** them came.  
> सही: **Each of** them came.

### 2. Each और Every के बाद Plural Nouns नहीं आते

> गलत: Each students must answer.  
> सही: Each student must answer.

> गलत: Every books has an index.  
> सही: Every book has an index.

### 3. Plural Subject के बाद Each

जब "each" plural subject के बाद आता है, verb real subject से agree करता है।

> The students **each have** an ID card.

यहाँ subject "students" है, इसलिए "have" correct है। लेकिन अगर "each" subject है, singular use करें:

> **Each** has an ID card.

### 4. Each/Every के बाद Pronouns

Traditional formal grammar often singular pronouns prefer करती है:

> Each student must bring **his or her** notebook.

Modern English में singular "they" भी common है:

> Each student must bring **their** notebook.

Exams में test की expected style follow करें। General writing में singular "they" widely accepted है।

## Common Mistakes

| गलत | सही | क्यों |
|---|---|---|
| Each students are ready. | Each student is ready. | Each + singular noun + singular verb. |
| Every of them came. | Every one of them came. | Every of नहीं, every one of. |
| Each and every are followed by plural nouns. | Each and every are followed by singular nouns. | इनके बाद noun normally singular होता है। |
| The teacher gave every a book. | The teacher gave each student a book. | Every को noun या every one/everyone चाहिए। |
| Every rooms have fans. | Every room has a fan. | Singular noun और verb. |
| Each of the answers are correct. | Each of the answers is correct. | Each of + plural object, but singular verb. |

## Quick Practice

   a) each  b) every of

   a) each  b) every

5. Each of the girls are wearing a badge.

6. Every of the rooms has a window.

Rewrite या fill in the blank.

8. Fill in: ___ of them knows the answer.

9. Correct rewrite करें: The teacher gave every a worksheet.

### Answer Key

1. **Each** — singular noun से पहले।  
2. **is** — every one singular है।  
3. **each** — individual attention.  
4. **every** — repeated time pattern.  
5. Each of the girls **is** wearing a badge.  
6. **Every one of** the rooms has a window. / **Each of** the rooms has a window.  
7. **Each student** must submit the assignment.  
8. **Each** — each of them knows.  
9. The teacher gave **each student** a worksheet.  
10. **every** — routine frequency.
