## Introduction: Impact के लिए Sentence को Flip करना

Normal English sentences subject-verb order follow करते हैं। Inversion इसे flip करता है: auxiliary verb subject से पहले आता है।

> Normal: I have **never** seen such a sight.  
> Inversion: **Never have I** seen such a sight.

Inversion कुछ negative या restrictive words के बाद sentence की शुरुआत में होता है। यह emphasis create करता है और formal writing और exams में common है।

---

## Core Meaning and Rule

> **Rule box:** Negative या restrictive fronting के बाद, **auxiliary + subject + main verb** use करें। अगर auxiliary न हो, तो *do/does/did* add करें।

| Trigger | Example |
|---|---|
| Never | **Never have I** seen this. |
| Rarely | **Rarely does he** complain. |
| Seldom | **Seldom do we** get such chances. |
| Not only | **Not only does she** sing, but she also dances. |
| Only then | **Only then did I** understand. |
| Hardly | **Hardly had I** arrived when it started raining. |
| No sooner | **No sooner had I** left than the phone rang. |
| Not until | **Not until later did she** realise. |
| Under no circumstances | **Under no circumstances should you** give up. |

---

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Never / Rarely / Seldom

> **Never have I** seen such dedication.  
> **Rarely does he** arrive on time.  
> **Seldom do we** witness such talent.

### 2. Not Only... But Also

> **Not only does she** sing, but she also dances.  
> **Not only was he** late, but he also forgot the documents.

Inversion पहले clause में होता है (*not only* के बाद)। दूसरा clause (*but also*) normal order follow करता है।

### 3. Only Then / Only After / Only When

> **Only then did I** understand the problem.  
> **Only after the meeting did she** explain her plan.  
> **Only when you try do you** learn.

### 4. Hardly / Scarcely / Barely... When

> **Hardly had I** arrived when the phone rang.  
> **Scarcely had she** left when it started raining.

Inversion clause में past perfect, *when* clause में simple past।

### 5. No Sooner... Than

> **No sooner had I** sat down than the doorbell rang.  
> **No sooner had they** arrived than the rain started.

Inversion clause में past perfect, *than* clause में simple past।

### 6. Not Until

> **Not until midnight did** he finish the report.  
> **Not until you see it will you** believe it.

### 7. Under No Circumstances / On No Account

> **Under no circumstances should you** share your password.  
> **On no account must you** open this door.

## Decision Checklist

1. **क्या sentence की शुरुआत में negative या restrictive expression है?** → Inversion ज़रूरी।
2. **क्या sentence में auxiliary है?** → उसे subject से पहले ले जाएं।
3. **No auxiliary?** → *Do/does/did* add करें और base verb use करें।
4. **Tense check करें।** Hardly/no sooner → past perfect। अन्य → original tense match करें।
5. **Emphasis के लिए पढ़ें।** Inverted form ज़्यादा formal और emphatic लगना चाहिए।

### Sample 1

> Never I have seen this.

Inversion ज़रूरी: auxiliary subject से पहले।

> **Never have I** seen this.

### Sample 2

> Only then I understood.

Original में कोई auxiliary नहीं → *did* add करें।

> **Only then did I** understand.

### Sample 3

> Not only she sings but also dances.

*Not only* के बाद inversion।

> **Not only does she** sing, but she also dances.

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Fronting के बाद No Inversion

> Wrong: Never **I have** seen this.  
> Right: Never **have I** seen this.

Negative fronting के बाद inversion ज़रूरी है।

### Trap 2: Do/Does/Did भूलना

> Wrong: Only then **I understood**.  
> Right: Only then **did I** understand.

Auxiliary न हो तो *do/does/did* add करें।

### Trap 3: Hardly/No Sooner में Wrong Tense

> Wrong: Hardly **I had** arrived when...  
> Right: Hardly **had I** arrived when...

Inversion + past perfect ज़रूरी।

### Trap 4: Not Only से दोनों Clauses Invert करना

> Wrong: Not only **does she** sing, but also **does she** dance.  
> Right: Not only **does she** sing, but she also **dances**.

सिर्फ पहला clause invert होता है।

### Trap 5: Indirect Questions में Inversion

> Wrong: He asked where **was I** going.  
> Right: He asked where **I was** going.

Reported/indirect questions में inversion नहीं होता।

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Right | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Never **I have** seen this. | Never **have I** seen this. | Inversion required. |
| Only then **I understood**. | Only then **did I** understand. | Add *did*. |
| Hardly **I had** arrived. | Hardly **had I** arrived. | Inversion + past perfect. |
| Not only **she sings**. | Not only **does she** sing. | Inversion after *not only*. |

## Quick Practice

Inversion से rewrite करें।

1. I have never seen such a beautiful place.
2. He rarely speaks in public.
3. I only understood the problem after the lecture.
4. She had hardly left when the phone rang.
5. Error spotting: Never I have heard such nonsense.
6. Error spotting: Only then I realised my mistake.
7. Error spotting: Not only she writes novels but also directs films.
8. Fill in the blank: No sooner ___ than the lights went out. (I / leave)
9. Rewrite: You should not share this under any circumstances.
10. Choose: Hardly ___ when the storm began. (had we left / we had left)

### Answer Key

1. **Never have I** seen such a beautiful place.  
2. **Rarely does he** speak in public.  
3. **Only after the lecture did I** understand the problem.  
4. **Hardly had she** left when the phone rang.  
5. **Never have I** heard such nonsense.  
6. **Only then did I** realise my mistake.  
7. **Not only does she** write novels, but she also directs films.  
8. **had I left** — inversion + past perfect.  
9. **Under no circumstances should you** share this.  
10. **had we left** — inversion.
