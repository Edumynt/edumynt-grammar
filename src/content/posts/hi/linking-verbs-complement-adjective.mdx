## परिचय: "She Looks Beautifully" गलत क्यों है

> ❌ She looks **beautifully**.  
> ✅ She looks **beautiful**.

> ❌ The food tastes **well**.  
> ✅ The food tastes **good**.

कुछ verbs action नहीं दिखाते। वे subject को किसी quality, state या identity से जोड़ते हैं। ऐसे verbs को **linking verbs** कहते हैं। Linking verbs के बाद subject को describe करने के लिए usually **adjective** आता है, adverb नहीं।

---

## मूल अर्थ और नियम

> **Rule box:** Linking verbs (*be, seem, become, appear, look, sound, taste, smell, feel, grow, turn, remain, stay*) subject को **subject complement** से जोड़ते हैं। Subject complement अगर quality बताता है, तो **adjective** use करें, adverb नहीं।

Pattern:

> Subject + linking verb + subject complement

Examples:

> She **is** intelligent.  
> He **seems** tired.  
> The soup **tastes** delicious.

इन sentences में complement subject के बारे में information देता है।

| Verb | Example |
|---|---|

| **become** | She **became** a doctor. |
| **appear** | He **appears** nervous. |
| **look** | She **looks** happy. |

## Adjective vs Adverb After Linking Verbs

Compare करें:

> She looks **beautiful**. *(adjective — "she" को describe करता है)*  
> She sings **beautifully**. *(adverb — "sings" action को describe करता है)*

> The food tastes **good**. *(adjective — "food" को describe करता है)*  
> He cooks **well**. *(adverb — "cooks" action को describe करता है)*

**Test:** अगर verb subject की condition, quality या state बता रहा है, adjective use करें। अगर verb action बता रहा है और word action को modify कर रहा है, adverb use करें।

> She **is** happy. *(happy = adjective, subject को describe करता है)*  
> She **works** happily. *(happily = adverb, action को describe करता है)*

## Subject Complement क्या होता है?

Subject complement वह word/phrase है जो linking verb के बाद आता है और subject को complete करता है।

> She is **happy**.  
> He became **a teacher**.  
> The room smells **fresh**.

यह complement subject के बारे में बताता है, object नहीं होता।

### Adjective Complement

> She seems **tired**.  
> The plan sounds **risky**.  
> He remained **silent**.

### Noun Complement

Linking verbs nouns भी ले सकते हैं:

> He **became** a teacher. *(noun complement)*  
> She **is** a doctor.  
> They **remained** friends.  
> He **turned** traitor.

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Right | Why |
|---|---|---|
| She looks beautifully. | She looks **beautiful**. | Linking verb के बाद adjective। |
| The food tastes well. | The food tastes **good**. | Food को describe करना है। |
| He feels badly about it. | He feels **bad** about it. | Feeling/state subject की है। |
| That sounds interestingly. | That sounds **interesting**. | Sound यहाँ linking verb है। |

**Note:** "He feels badly" का rare meaning हो सकता है कि उसकी touch करने की ability खराब है। लेकिन "He feels bad" का normal meaning है कि वह दुखी/गिल्टी महसूस कर रहा है।

## Exam Traps और Usage Notes

### Trap 1: हर -ly word को correct समझना

Adverbs अक्सर -ly पर end होते हैं, लेकिन linking verb के बाद adjective चाहिए।

> ❌ The idea sounds nicely.  
> ✅ The idea sounds nice.

### Trap 2: Good vs Well

**Good** adjective है; **well** usually adverb है।

> The soup tastes **good**.  
> He writes **well**.

लेकिन health के meaning में **well** adjective भी हो सकता है:

> I am well. *(healthy)*

### Trap 3: Same verb action भी हो सकता है

कुछ verbs context के अनुसार linking या action verb बन सकते हैं।

> She **looked** happy. *(linking — appeared happy)*  
> She **looked** carefully at the painting. *(action — looked carefully)*

> The dog **smelled** bad. *(linking — dog की smell bad थी)*  
> The dog **smelled** the food carefully. *(action — smelling action)*

## Quick Practice

Correct form चुनें।

1. She looks ___. *(beautiful/beautifully)*
2. The soup tastes ___. *(good/well)*
3. He seems ___. *(happy/happily)*
4. Error spotting: She looks beautifully.
5. Error spotting: The food tastes well.
6. Fill in: He ___ a teacher. *(is/became)*
7. Fill in: The fabric feels ___. *(soft/softly)*
8. Rewrite correctly: She sings beautiful.
9. Choose: He remained ___. *(calm/calmly)*
10. Choose: That sounds ___. *(interesting/interestingly)*

### Answer Key

1. **beautiful** — linking verb के बाद adjective।
2. **good** — soup को describe करता है।
3. **happy** — subject complement।
4. **She looks beautiful.**
5. **The food tastes good.**
6. **is / became** — दोनों noun complement के साथ correct हो सकते हैं।
7. **soft** — fabric को describe करता है।
8. **She sings beautifully.** — यहाँ sing action verb है, इसलिए adverb।
9. **calm** — adjective।
10. **interesting** — adjective।

## Summary

> **Rule:** Linking verbs subject को complement से जोड़ते हैं। Quality बताने के लिए इनके बाद **adjective** आता है, adverb नहीं। Adjective subject को describe करता है; adverb action को describe करता है।

Memory trick: **Linking verbs link to the subject. Adjectives describe subjects. Adverbs describe actions.**
