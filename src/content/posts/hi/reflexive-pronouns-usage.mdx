## परिचय: "Myself Keshav" ग़लत है

English में सबसे आम pronoun errors में से एक:

> ❌ **Myself** Keshav.  
> ✅ **I am** Keshav.

> ❌ Rahul and **myself** went to the store.  
> ✅ Rahul and **I** went to the store.

Reflexive pronouns (*myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves*) regular pronouns के fancy replacements नहीं हैं। इनके specific uses हैं — और इन्हें ग़लत उपयोग करना poor grammar का सबूत है।

---

## मूल अर्थ और नियम

> **Rule box:** Reflexive pronouns उपयोग करें **(1)** जब subject और object एक हों, **(2)** emphasis के लिए (intensive use), या **(3)** fixed expressions में। *I, me, he, she* आदि के substitute के रूप में कभी मत उपयोग करें।

| उपयोग | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| Subject = Object | I hurt **myself**. |
| Emphasis | She **herself** completed the project. |
| Fixed expression | Help **yourself**. / Be **yourself**. |

---

## रूप, पैटर्न और वाक्य में स्थिति

### 1. Reflexion Pronoun Set

| Subject Pronoun | Reflexive Pronoun |
|---|---|
| I | myself |
| you (singular) | yourself |
| he | himself |
| she | herself |
| it | itself |
| we | ourselves |
| you (plural) | yourselves |
| they | themselves |

### 2. जब Subject = Object (True Reflexive)

Reflexive pronoun उपयोग करें जब subject और object एक ही व्यक्ति या चीज़ को refer करें।

> I hurt **myself**. *(I = myself)*  
> She blamed **herself** for the mistake. *(she = herself)*  
> He taught **himself** to play guitar. *(he = himself)*  
> They enjoyed **themselves** at the party. *(they = themselves)*

### 3. Emphatic (Intensive) Use

Reflexive pronoun subject के बाद (या clause के अंत में) emphasis जोड़ने के लिए उपयोग हो सकता है।

> I **myself** saw the accident. *(emphasis: मैंने खुद)*  
> She **herself** completed the entire project. *(emphasis: उसने, किसी और ने नहीं)*  
> The president **himself** attended the meeting.

### 4. Fixed Expressions

कई common expressions reflexive pronouns उपयोग करती हैं:

> Help **yourself**. *(जो चाहें ले लें)*  
> Be **yourself**. *(स्वाभाविक रहें)*  
> Make **yourself** at home. *(आराम करें)*  
> She lives by **herself**. *(अकेले)*  
> They did it **themselves**. *(बिना मदद के)*

### 5. Common Errors

**Error 1: I/me के बजाय myself उपयोग करना**

> ❌ **Myself** Keshav.  
> ✅ **I am** Keshav.

> ❌ Please contact **myself** if you have questions.  
> ✅ Please contact **me** if you have questions.

**Error 2: Reflexive ज़रूरत होने पर regular pronoun उपयोग करना**

> ❌ I hurt **me**.  
> ✅ I hurt **myself**.

> ❌ She looked at **her** in the mirror. *(ambiguous)*  
> ✅ She looked at **herself** in the mirror.

## Decision Checklist

1. **क्या subject object के बराबर है?** → Reflexive उपयोग करें।
2. **क्या आप emphasis जोड़ रहे हैं?** → Subject के बाद reflexive उपयोग करें।
3. **क्या यह fixed expression है?** → Reflexive form उपयोग करें।
4. **क्या आप formal लगना चाहते हैं?** → *Me/I* के बजाय *myself* मत उपयोग करें। यह बेहतर नहीं, ग़लत लगता है।
5. **क्या आप reflexive को हटा सकते हैं बिना core meaning बदले?** → यह emphatic है, essential नहीं।

## अंतर दिखाने वाले उदाहरण

1. I hurt **myself**. *(subject = object)*
2. She **herself** completed the project. *(emphasis)*
3. Help **yourself**. *(fixed expression)*
4. They enjoyed **themselves**. *(subject = object)*
5. The president **himself** was present. *(emphasis)*
6. He taught **himself** to code. *(subject = object)*
7. Make **yourself** at home. *(fixed expression)*
8. We **ourselves** are to blame. *(emphasis)*
9. She looked at **herself** in the mirror. *(subject = object)*
10. I am not **myself** today. *(fixed expression)*

## Exam Traps और Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Subject की तरह "Myself"

> ❌ **Myself** and Rahul went to the store.  
> ✅ **Rahul and I** went to the store.

*Myself* कभी sentence का subject नहीं हो सकता।

### Trap 2: Prepositions के बाद "Myself" (जब Reflexive नहीं)

> ❌ Send it to **myself**.  
> ✅ Send it to **me**.

Prepositions के बाद *me* उपयोग करें जब तक subject *I* न हो।

### Trap 3: Emphatic Position

> ✅ **I myself** saw it. *(subject के बाद)*  
> ✅ I saw it **myself**. *(verb के बाद — भी मान्य)*  
> ❌ **Myself**, I saw it. *(ग़लत position)*

## आम गलतियाँ

| ग़लत | सही | क्यों |
|---|---|---|
| Myself Keshav. | I am Keshav. | Myself subject नहीं हो सकता। |
| Rahul and myself went. | Rahul and I went. | I उपयोग करें, myself नहीं। |
| Send it to myself. | Send it to me. | कोई reflexive antecedent नहीं। |
| I hurt me. | I hurt myself. | Subject = object → reflexive। |

6. Error spotting: I cut me while cooking.

### Answer Key

1. **me** — कोई reflexive antecedent नहीं।  
2. **herself** — emphasis।  
3. **I** — subject pronoun।  
4. **I am responsible for this.** — myself subject नहीं हो सकता।  
5. **Please send the email to me.** — कोई reflexive antecedent नहीं।  
6. **I cut myself while cooking.** — subject = object।  
7. **themselves** — subject = object।  
8. **himself** — emphasis।  
9. **Between you and me, this is a bad idea.** — preposition के बाद object pronoun।  
10. **himself** — subject = object।

## सारांश

> **Rule:** Reflexive pronouns उपयोग करें जब subject = object, emphasis के लिए, या fixed expressions में। *I* या *me* के substitute के रूप में *myself* कभी मत उपयोग करें — यह ज़्यादा formal नहीं, ग़लत है।

Memory trick: **"Myself reflect करता है। यह subject की तरह bounce करता है। अगर bounce करने के लिए कोई subject नहीं है, तो उपयोग मत करें।"**

ये revise करें:

- I hurt **myself**. *(subject = object)*  
- She **herself** completed it. *(emphasis)*  
- Help **yourself**. *(fixed expression)*  
- Rahul and **I** went. *(not "myself")*
