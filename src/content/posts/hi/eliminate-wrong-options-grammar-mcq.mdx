## Introduction: Elimination Selection से Better है

ज्यादातर students grammar MCQ में right answer ढूंढने की कोशिश करते हैं। Strong test-takers उसके उल्टा करते हैं — वे पहले wrong options हटाते हैं।

> Question: Each of the boys ___ a pen.  
> a) have b) has c) having d) were

आपको पूरा rule तुरंत नहीं पता होना चाहिए। आप देख सकते हैं कि **having** finite verb नहीं है और **were** singular subject से match नहीं करता। बचा **has**। दो options eliminated, answer एक step दूर।

यह article आपको elimination patterns सिखाता है जो grammar MCQs में काम करते हैं। Related technique के लिए [How to Solve Fill in the Blanks Using Grammar Clues](/hi/posts/fill-in-blanks-grammar-clues/) देखें।

## Core Meaning and Rule

MCQ elimination का मतलब है grammar rule तोड़ने वाले options को reject करना, भले ही आपको final answer पर 100% sure न हो। ज्यादातर grammar MCQs में दो obviously wrong options, एक close distractor, और एक correct answer होता है।

मुख्य elimination filters ये हैं:

- **Agreement filter** — क्या verb subject से number में match करता है?
- **Tense filter** — क्या verb time marker से match करता है?
- **Article filter** — क्या article उस sound से match करता है जो उसके बाद आता है?
- **Negation filter** — क्या sentence standard English में double negatives से बचता है?

> **Rule box:** Close options में choose करने से पहले grammar rules से impossible choices हटाएं।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Agreement Elimination

अगर subject singular है, plural verbs reject करें। अगर subject plural है, singular verbs reject करें।

> Every student ___ present.  
> a) are b) is c) were d) have

**Every** subject को singular बनाता है। **Are**, **were**, और **have** reject करें। सिर्फ **is** बचता है।

| Subject cue | Reject |
|---|---|
| each / every / neither + noun | plural verbs |
| plural subject without each/every | singular verbs |
| collective noun (formal) | plural verbs (usually) |

### 2. Tense Elimination

Time words तुरंत tense options eliminate करते हैं।

> She ___ yesterday.  
> a) has arrived b) arrives c) arrived d) will arrive

**Yesterday** finished past time है। **Has arrived** (present perfect), **arrives** (present), और **will arrive** (future) reject करें। सिर्फ **arrived** fit होता है।

| Time marker | Reject |
|---|---|
| yesterday, ago, last year | present perfect, present, future |
| since, for (with present perfect) | simple past |
| tomorrow, next week | past tenses |

### 3. Article Elimination

Articles sound पर depend करते हैं, spelling पर नहीं।

> ___ honest man  
> a) a b) an

**Honest** silent *h* से शुरू होता है, इसलिए पहला sound vowel है। **A** reject करें। **An** चुनें।

| First sound | Correct article |
|---|---|
| vowel sound | an |
| consonant sound | a |

Common traps: **an** honest man (silent h), **a** university (yoo- sound), **an** hour (silent h), **a** European (yoo- sound)।

### 4. Double Negative Elimination

Standard English में एक negative meaning express करने के लिए दो negatives use नहीं होते।

> I do not need ___ help.  
> a) no b) any

**Do not** पहले ही negative है। **No** reject करें (double negative)। **Any** चुनें।

| Already negative | Reject |
|---|---|
| do not / did not / cannot + no | double negative |
| neither / never / hardly + no | double negative |

## Decision Checklist for MCQ Elimination

1. **Full sentence पढ़ें।** पहले options न देखें।
2. **Grammar point identify करें।** Agreement? Tense? Article? Preposition? Negation?
3. **Rule mentally apply करें।** Answer कैसा दिखना चाहिए?
4. **Options scan करें।** जो rule तोड़ते हैं reject करें।
5. **Remaining options compare करें।** अगर दो बचें, meaning, register या collocation check करें।
6. **Best answer चुनें।**

### Sample 1

> Each of the girls ___ a chair.  
> a) have b) has c) having d) were

- **Having** finite verb नहीं है → reject।
- **Were** plural past → reject (subject singular है)।
- **Have** plural → reject।
- **Has** singular → correct।

### Sample 2

> She ___ to school yesterday.  
> a) has gone b) goes c) went d) will go

- **Yesterday** → finished past time।
- **Has gone**, **goes**, **will go** reject।
- **Went** simple past → correct।

### Sample 3

> He is ___ honest person.  
> a) a b) an

- **Honest** vowel sound से शुरू होता है (silent h)।
- **A** reject।
- **An** correct।

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. Every student **has** a book. *(singular agreement)*
2. She **went** yesterday. *(finished past time)*
3. **An** hour ago. *(vowel sound)*
4. I do not need **any** help. *(no double negative)*
5. Neither of them **is** ready. *(singular pronoun)*
6. **A** university student. *(consonant sound "yoo")*
7. They **have** completed the work. *(plural subject)*
8. **An** MBA degree. *(vowel sound "em")*
9. He **has lived** here since 2020. *(present perfect + since)*
10. I **submitted** it last night. *(simple past + finished time)*

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Verb के पास Plural Noun

> Wrong: Each of the boys **have** done it.  
> Right: Each of the boys **has** done it.

Plural noun **boys** verb के पास बैठा है, लेकिन **each** subject है। Plural verbs eliminate करें।

### Trap 2: Articles में Sound vs Spelling

> Wrong: **A** honest man.  
> Right: **An** honest man.

Sound पर जाएं, spelling पर नहीं। **Honest** vowel sound से शुरू होता है।

### Trap 3: Formal English में Double Negative

> Wrong: I do not know **nothing**.  
> Right: I do not know **anything**.

दो negatives standard English में एक दूसरे को cancel करते हैं या non-standard माने जाते हैं। दूसरा negative eliminate करें।

### Trap 4: Finished Time के साथ Present Perfect

> Wrong: I **have seen** him yesterday.  
> Right: I **saw** him yesterday.

**Yesterday** present perfect को eliminate करता है।

### Trap 5: Confusing Collocations

> Wrong: She is capable **to do** it.  
> Right: She is capable **of doing** it.

Adjective **capable** **of + gerund** लेता है, infinitive नहीं। Infinitive option eliminate करें।

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Right | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Each of the students **have** passed. | Each of the students **has** passed. | **Each** singular है। |
| I do not need **no** help. | I do not need **any** help. | Double negative। |
| **A** honest man. | **An** honest man. | Vowel sound। |
| She **has gone** yesterday. | She **went** yesterday. | Finished past time। |

## Quick Practice

Elimination से best answer चुनें।

1. Every child ___ a uniform.  
   a) have b) has c) having d) were
2. He ___ to Delhi last week.  
   a) has gone b) goes c) went d) will go
3. She is ___ M.A. graduate.  
   a) a b) an
4. I cannot find ___ book.  
   a) no b) any
5. Error spotting: Each of the members have signed.
6. Error spotting: I have completed it last Monday.
7. Error spotting: He is a honest boy.
8. Fill in the blank: ___ European country (a / an)
9. Rewrite correctly: I do not want no trouble.
10. Choose: She is capable ___ solving this. (of / to)

### Answer Key

1. **has** — **every** singular है।  
2. **went** — **last week** finished past time है।  
3. **an** — **M** vowel sound "em" से शुरू होता है।  
4. **any** — **cannot** पहले ही negative है।  
5. **Each of the members has signed.**  
6. **I completed it last Monday.**  
7. **He is an honest boy.**  
8. **A** — **European** "yoo" sound से शुरू होता है।  
9. **I do not want any trouble.**  
10. **of** — capable + of + gerund।
