## परिचय: तीन Prepositions, एक Logic

> She lives **in** India. (वह भारत में रहती है।)

> She lives **on** Main Street. (वह मेन स्ट्रीट पर रहती है।)

> She lives **at** 42 Main Street. (वह मेन स्ट्रीट के 42 नंबर पर रहती है।)

तीन prepositions। तीन levels of specificity। Logic एक है: **general → specific → precise point।**

यह scale — **in → on → at** — **place** और **time** दोनों पर लागू होता है। एक बार scale समझ लें, तो आप लगभग हर situation में ये तीन prepositions सही ढंग से इस्तेमाल कर सकते हैं।

## Place: General-to-Specific Scale

### "In" — सबसे Broad / Enclosed

सबसे बड़े areas और enclosed spaces के लिए "in" इस्तेमाल करें।

| Category | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| देश | in India, in Japan, in Brazil |
| शहर | in Delhi, in London, in Mumbai |
| कमरे | in the kitchen, in the bedroom |
| Enclosed spaces | in the box, in the car, in the garden |
| पानी | in the pool, in the river |
| किताबें | in the book, in the newspaper |

> She works **in** a bank.

> The keys are **in** my pocket.

### "On" — Surface, Street, Floor

Surfaces, streets, floors, और चीज़ों के लिए जो surface पर हों।

| Category | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| Surfaces | on the table, on the wall, on the floor |
| सड़कें | on Main Street, on the highway |
| मंज़िलें | on the 3rd floor, on the ground floor |
| Public transport | on the bus, on the train, on the plane |
| तरफ़ | on the left, on the right |

> The book is **on** the table.

> She was **on** the bus when I called.

### "At" — Specific Point

Precise points, specific addresses, और particular locations के लिए।

| Category | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| Specific addresses | at 42 Main Street |
| Specific locations | at the bus stop, at the door, at the entrance |
| घटनाएँ | at the party, at the meeting, at the concert |
| Home/work/school | at home, at work, at school |
| स्थिति | at the top, at the bottom, at the corner |

> I'll meet you **at** the bus stop.

> She is **at** home right now.

### Scale in Action

> She is **in** the hospital. (इमारत के अंदर — general)

> She is **at** the hospital. (उस location पर — specific point)

> She is **on** the third floor of the hospital. (surface/floor level)

## Time: वही Scale

### "In" — सबसे Broad Time Periods

| Category | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| महीने | in June, in December |
| वर्ष | in 2020, in 1995 |
| मौसम | in winter, in summer |
| शताब्दियाँ | in the 21st century |
| दिन के भाग | in the morning, in the afternoon, in the evening |
| लंबी अवधि | in the past, in the future, in a few minutes |

> I was born **in** 1990.

> We have exams **in** March.

> She likes to exercise **in** the morning.

### "On" — दिन और तारीख़ें

| Category | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| सप्ताह के दिन | on Monday, on Friday |
| तारीख़ें | on 15th August, on January 1st |
| खास दिन | on my birthday, on Christmas Day |
| Specific दिन के भाग | on Monday morning, on the night of the 5th |

> The meeting is **on** Tuesday.

> India became independent **on** 15th August 1947.

### "At" — Precise Times

| Category | उदाहरण |
|---|---|
| Clock times | at 5 PM, at noon, at midnight |
| भोजन का समय | at breakfast, at lunch, at dinner |
| Specific moments | at that time, at the moment, at sunrise |
| रात | at night |
| उम्र | at 18, at the age of 60 |

> The train leaves **at** 6:30.

> I usually have lunch **at** 1 PM.

> She went to bed **at** midnight.

### Scale in Action (Time)

> **In** 2020 (वर्ष — broad), **on** 15th August (तारीख़ — specific day), **at** 10:00 AM (clock time — precise moment).

## "At Home" Exception

कुछ fixed expressions में "at" इस्तेमाल होता है:

> at home, at work, at school, at university

आप "in home" या "on home" नहीं कहते। बस याद रखें: **at home, at work, at school**।

## Transport: "In" बनाम "On"

### "On" — बड़ी Public Transport

जिनपर आप खड़े होकर चल सकते हैं:

> on the bus, on the train, on the plane, on the ship, on the metro

### "In" — छोटी / Private Vehicles

जिनमें आप enclosed बैठते हैं:

> in the car, in a taxi, in a helicopter, in a small boat

### Logic

Bus या train पर आप खड़े हो सकते हैं और चल सकते हैं — आप उसके "on" हैं। Car में आप enclosed बैठते हैं — आप उसके "in" हैं।

## आम गलतियाँ

### गलती 1: "In Monday"

गलत: ~~The meeting is in Monday.~~

सही: **The meeting is on Monday.**

दिनों में "on" आता है।

### गलती 2: "At June"

गलत: ~~I was born at June.~~

सही: **I was born in June.**

महीनों में "in" आता है।

### गलती 3: "In the bus" बनाम "On the bus"

> She is **on** the bus. (public transport — सही)

> She is **in** the car. (private vehicle — सही)

Public transport के लिए "on" इस्तेमाल करें।

### गलती 4: "In night"

गलत: ~~I work in night.~~

सही: **I work at night.**

"Night" के साथ "at" आता है। यह fixed expression है।

### गलती 5: "At the table" बनाम "On the table"

इनका अर्थ अलग है:

> She sat **at** the table. (स्थिति — मेज़ के पास/बैठकर)

> The book is **on** the table. (surface — किताब मेज़ के ऊपर)

## अभ्यास प्रश्न

### रिक्त स्थान भरें

1. The meeting is ______ Monday ______ 3 PM.
2. She lives ______ New York ______ 5th Avenue.
3. I was born ______ 1998.
4. The cat is sleeping ______ the sofa. (surface)
5. We arrived ______ the airport ______ time.

### त्रुटि खोजें

6. I will see you in Friday evening.
7. She is waiting on the bus stop.
8. He lives at the third floor.
9. The picture is at the wall.
10. We usually have dinner in 8 o'clock.

### उत्तर कुंजी

1. **on** Monday **at** 3 PM — दिन में "on," clock time में "at"
2. **in** New York **on** 5th Avenue — शहर में "in," सड़क में "on"
3. **in** 1998 — वर्ष में "in"
4. **on** the sofa — surface
5. **at** the airport **on** time — specific location में "at"; "on time" fixed expression
6. **on** Friday evening — दिन में "on"
7. **at** the bus stop — specific point में "at"
8. **on** the third floor — floor level में "on"
9. **on** the wall — surface में "on"
10. **at** 8 o'clock — clock time में "at"

## सारांऩ

| | In | On | At |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Place** | Enclosed / बड़ा area (in India, in the room) | Surface / सड़क (on the table, on Main Street) | Specific point (at the door, at 42 Main Street) |
| **Time** | Broad period (in June, in 2020, in the morning) | दिन / तारीख़ (on Monday, on 15th August) | Precise time (at 5 PM, at night, at noon) |

जब शक हो, सोचें: **general → surface → point**।
