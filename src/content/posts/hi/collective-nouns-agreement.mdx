## Introduction: "The Team Is" या "The Team Are"?

Collective nouns English agreement का tricky topic हैं, क्योंकि context के हिसाब से दोनों forms correct हो सकते हैं।

> सही: The team **is** winning.  
> British English में सही: The team **are** arguing among themselves.

**Team** एक group को name करता है। कभी हम group को one unit की तरह देखते हैं। कभी हम group के individual members पर focus करते हैं। यही difference verb choice को affect करता है, especially British English में।

Exams में safest answer sentence meaning और expected variety of English पर depend करता है। Formal American English collective nouns के साथ usually singular agreement prefer करता है। British English members पर focus होने पर plural agreement ज्यादा freely allow करता है।

---

## Core Meaning and Rule

**Collective noun** वह noun है जो people, animals या things के group को one unit की तरह name करता है। Common collective nouns:

- **team**
- **family**
- **committee**
- **class**
- **staff**
- **government**
- **audience**
- **jury**
- **crowd**
- **company**

> **Rule box:** अगर group one unit की तरह act कर रहा है, singular verb use करें। British English में अगर individual members पर focus है, plural verb possible है। American English collective nouns के साथ generally singular agreement ज्यादा prefer करता है।

Compare:

> **The committee has** approved its report.  
> **The committee are** divided in their opinions. *(BrE)*

पहले sentence में committee official body की तरह है। दूसरे में members अलग-अलग opinions रखते हैं।

## Main Agreement Patterns

| Meaning Focus | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| group as one unit | collective noun + singular verb | **The team is** winning. |
| members as individuals | collective noun + plural verb *(mainly BrE)* | **The team are** arguing. |
| official action | singular verb | **The committee has** approved the plan. |
| internal disagreement | plural verb in BrE | **The committee are** divided. |
| size/identity of group | singular verb | **My family is** large. |

### Group as One Unit

जब group एक whole unit की तरह act करे, singular agreement use करें:

> **The class is** ready for the test.  
> **The audience was** silent.  
> **The company has** opened a new branch.

यहाँ focus separate members पर नहीं, whole group पर है।

### Members as Individuals

British English में जब members अलग-अलग action कर रहे हों, plural agreement natural हो सकता है:

> **The family are** sitting in different rooms.  
> **The staff are** arguing about the schedule.  
> **The jury are** unable to agree.

American English में इन्हीं sentences को often singular या rephrased form में लिखा जाता है:

> The family members are sitting in different rooms.  
> The staff members are arguing about the schedule.

## BrE vs AmE: Exam में क्या करें?

British English collective nouns के साथ notional agreement use करता है: meaning unit हो तो singular, members हों तो plural।

American English grammatical agreement को ज्यादा strongly follow करता है: collective noun singular form में है, इसलिए singular verb preferred होता है।

| Context | British English | American English |
|---|---|---|
| group as unit | The team **is** ready. | The team **is** ready. |
| members emphasized | The team **are** changing shirts. | The team members **are** changing shirts. |
| official body | The committee **has** decided. | The committee **has** decided. |
| divided members | The committee **are** divided. | The committee members **are** divided. |

Exam में अगर variety specified नहीं है, singular agreement usually safer है unless sentence clearly tests British notional agreement or phrase uses **members**.

## Pronoun Agreement

Verb के साथ pronoun भी meaning और variety से affected हो सकता है।

> The team has won **its** first match.  
> The team are wearing **their** new jerseys. *(BrE, members focus)*

Formal writing में consistency रखें:

- Singular verb + singular pronoun: **The committee has submitted its report.**
- Plural verb + plural pronoun: **The committee are discussing their views.** *(BrE)*

Mixed form awkward लग सकता है:

> Awkward: The committee **has** submitted **their** report.

Modern English में singular **they** valid है for people, but collective noun agreement questions में exam usually consistency expect करता है।

## Decision Checklist

1. Collective noun identify करें: team, family, committee, staff, audience, jury आदि।
2. पूछें: group one unit की तरह act कर रहा है या members separately?
3. अगर one unit है, singular verb use करें.
4. अगर members separately हैं और British English context है, plural verb possible है.
5. American/formal exam context में singular agreement usually safer है; members focus चाहिए तो **members** add करके sentence clearer बनाएं.
6. Pronoun agreement consistent रखें: **its** with singular, **their** with plural members focus.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Better Sentence | Why |
|---|---|---|
| The team **are** winning. *(AmE/formal exam)* | The team **is** winning. | Team one unit है। |
| The committee **have** approved its report. | The committee **has** approved its report. | Official body singular है। |
| The family **is** sitting in different rooms. | The family members **are** sitting in different rooms. | Members focus clearer है। |
| The jury **is** divided in their views. | The jury **is** divided in its view / The jury members **are** divided. | Agreement consistent रखें। |

## Exam Traps

### Trap 1: Collective Noun देखकर Always Plural चुनना

> गलत: The class **are** ready for the test.  
> बेहतर: The class **is** ready for the test.

Class एक whole unit की तरह ready है।

### Trap 2: British Usage को Universal Rule मान लेना

British English में plural agreement possible है, लेकिन every context में नहीं। Official action या unit focus में singular verb अभी भी common है।

### Trap 3: Long Phrase से Head Noun Confuse होना

> The team of young players **is** confident.

Head noun **team** है, **players** नहीं। इसलिए singular verb natural है।

### Trap 4: Pronoun Inconsistency

> The committee **has** given **their** decision.

Formal exam में better:

> The committee **has** given **its** decision.

## Mini Summary

- Collective nouns name a group: **team, family, committee, class, staff, audience**.
- Unit focus हो तो singular verb: **The team is ready**.
- Member focus हो तो British English में plural verb possible: **The team are arguing**.
- American/formal exam English usually singular agreement prefer करता है unless sentence is rephrased with **members**.
- Pronoun agreement consistent रखें: **its** for unit, **their** for members.
- Real head noun identify करें; nearby plural words से confuse न हों।

## FAQ

### क्या "team are" गलत है?

British English में गलत नहीं, अगर team members पर focus है। American English और many formal exam contexts में **team is** safer होता है।

### क्या collective noun हमेशा singular होता है?

Form में collective noun singular हो सकता है, लेकिन British English meaning के हिसाब से plural agreement allow कर सकता है।

### Exam में कौन सा form चुनना चाहिए?

अगर context clear नहीं है, singular verb generally safer है। अगर sentence members acting separately दिखाता है और British English context है, plural verb possible है।

### "family is" या "family are"?

दोनों context पर depend करते हैं। **My family is large** में family one unit है। **My family are arriving at different times** British English में members focus है।
