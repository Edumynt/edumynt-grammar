## परिचय: "I Suggest That He Go" में *goes* क्यों नहीं?

Formal English में कुछ verbs और expressions के बाद verb की **base form** आती है — यानी न *-s*, न *to*, न tense marker:

> ✅ I suggest that he **go** to the doctor.  
> ❌ I suggest that he **goes** to the doctor.

> ✅ It is important that she **be** present.  
> ❌ It is important that she **is** present.

यह structure **subjunctive mood** कहलाता है। English में mood बताता है कि sentence fact बता रहा है, command दे रहा है, possibility दिखा रहा है, या hypothetical/unreal situation बता रहा है। Subjunctive especially demands, suggestions, recommendations और unreal hypotheticals में दिखता है।

---

## मूल अर्थ और नियम

> **Rule box:** Demands, suggestions, recommendations और necessity वाले expressions के बाद **that + subject + base verb** प्रयोग करें। Hypothetical situations में **were** सभी subjects के साथ formal choice है: *If I were*, *if he were*, *if she were*.

Subjunctive का मतलब यह नहीं कि action already हुआ है। यह अक्सर बताता है कि कोई action demanded, suggested, required, imagined या unreal है।

## Forms, Patterns और Sentence Position

### 1. Mandative Subjunctive: demand, suggestion, recommendation

इन verbs/phrases के बाद base verb आता है:

| Trigger | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| suggest | suggest that + subject + base verb | I suggest that he **study**. |
| insist | insist that + subject + base verb | She insisted that they **be** punctual. |
| recommend | recommend that + subject + base verb | The doctor recommended that he **rest**. |
| demand | demand that + subject + base verb | They demanded that the manager **apologise**. |
| essential/important/necessary | It is important that + subject + base verb | It is important that she **attend**. |

ध्यान दें: third-person singular subject होने पर भी *goes/studies/is* नहीं आएगा। Subjunctive में verb base form रहता है।

### 2. British और American English

American English में mandative subjunctive अधिक common है:

> AmE: I suggest that he **study** harder.

British English में कई बार **should + base verb** भी natural लगता है:

> BrE: I suggest that he **should study** harder.

Exam और formal writing में direct subjunctive form सुरक्षित choice है, especially जब question grammar rule test कर रहा हो।

### 3. Were-Subjunctive: unreal/hypothetical situations

Unreal या imaginary situations में **were** formal form है:

> If I **were** you, I would apologise.  
> If he **were** here, he would explain it.  
> I wish she **were** more careful.  
> Suppose it **were** true, what would happen?

Informal speech में *If I was you* सुनने को मिल सकता है, लेकिन formal grammar और exams में **If I were you** बेहतर है।

### 4. Formulaic Subjunctive: fixed expressions

कुछ fixed expressions पुरानी subjunctive form रखते हैं:

> **God save** the King.  
> **Come** what may.  
> **Be** that as it may.  
> **Suffice it to say**.  
> If need **be**.

इनमें normal subject-verb agreement लगाकर *God saves* जैसा form नहीं बनाया जाता।

## Decision Checklist

1. **क्या sentence में suggest, insist, recommend, demand, require, propose, urge जैसा trigger है?** → base verb लगाएँ।
2. **क्या phrase है: it is important/necessary/essential that...?** → base verb लगाएँ।
3. **क्या situation unreal/hypothetical है?** → formal English में **were** चुनें।
4. **क्या expression fixed है?** → memorised subjunctive form रखें।
5. **क्या option में -s वाला verb और base verb दोनों हैं?** → trigger देखकर base verb चुनें।

Example:

> The teacher recommended that Rahul ___ daily. *(practises/practise)*  
> Trigger = recommended that → answer: **practise**.

## अंतर दिखाने वाले उदाहरण

1. I suggest that he **go** now. *(not goes)*
2. It is essential that every student **be** present. *(not is)*
3. The committee demanded that the report **be** revised. *(passive base form)*
4. She insisted that they **arrive** on time. *(not arrive/arrives confusion: base form)*
5. If I **were** you, I would wait. *(formal hypothetical)*
6. I wish he **were** more honest. *(unreal wish)*
7. **God save** the King. *(fixed expression)*
8. The doctor recommended that she **rest** for a week. *(not rests)*
9. It is necessary that the file **reach** the office today. *(not reaches)*
10. Suppose it **were** false — how would your answer change? *(hypothetical)*

## Exam Traps और Usage Notes

### Trap 1: third-person subject देखकर -s लगा देना

> ❌ I suggest that he **goes**.  
> ✅ I suggest that he **go**.

यह present simple नहीं है; यह subjunctive है।

### Trap 2: *is/are* के स्थान पर **be**

> ❌ It is important that she **is** present.  
> ✅ It is important that she **be** present.

Subjunctive में *be* की base form ही आती है।

### Trap 3: If I was you

> ❌ If I **was** you, I would study harder.  
> ✅ If I **were** you, I would study harder.

Informal speech अलग हो सकती है, लेकिन formal answer में **were** लिखें।

### Trap 4: fixed expression को normal grammar से बदलना

> ❌ God **saves** the King.  
> ✅ God **save** the King.

यह ordinary statement नहीं, formulaic expression है।

## Quick Practice

सही option चुनें या sentence correct करें।

1. I suggest that he ___ early. *(arrives/arrive)*
2. It is essential that she ___ present. *(is/be)*
3. If I ___ you, I would not ignore this. *(was/were)*
4. The doctor recommended that he ___ for two days. *(rests/rest)*
5. Error spotting: She insisted that Rahul goes with them.
6. Error spotting: It is necessary that every member is informed.
7. Error spotting: God saves the Queen.
8. Fill in the blank: I wish it ___ easier. *(was/were)*
9. Rewrite: The manager demanded that the employee apologises.
10. Choose: Come what ___, we will continue. *(may/might)*

### Answer Key

1. **arrive** — suggest that के बाद base verb।
2. **be** — essential that + base verb।
3. **were** — formal hypothetical।
4. **rest** — recommended that + base verb।
5. **She insisted that Rahul go with them.**
6. **It is necessary that every member be informed.**
7. **God save the Queen.**
8. **were** — wish + unreal situation।
9. **The manager demanded that the employee apologise.**
10. **may** — fixed expression: come what may।
