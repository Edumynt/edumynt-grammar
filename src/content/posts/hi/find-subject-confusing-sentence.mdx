## Introduction: Subject हमेशा Nearest Noun नहीं होता

Short sentences में subject usually easy होता है।

> The boy **runs** fast.

लेकिन real grammar questions इतने simple नहीं रहते। वे prepositional phrases, relative clauses, long descriptions और inverted word order add कर देते हैं। फिर students often real subject की जगह nearest noun चुन लेते हैं।

> Wrong: The quality of the products **are** poor.  
> Right: The quality of the products **is** poor.

Verb से पहले nearest noun **products** है, लेकिन sentence mainly products के बारे में नहीं है। Sentence **the quality** के बारे में है। **Quality** singular है, इसलिए verb **is** होगा।

Subject correctly find करना subject-verb agreement, tense choice, pronoun reference और sentence correction की base skill है। Subject wrong हुआ, तो बाद की grammar decisions भी shaky हो जाती हैं।

Related topics के लिए [How to Identify the Main Verb in a Long Sentence](/hi/posts/identify-main-verb/) और [Subject-Verb Agreement with One Of](/hi/posts/subject-verb-agreement-one-of/) revise करें।

## Core Meaning and Rule

**Subject** वह person, thing, idea या clause है जिसके बारे में sentence कुछ कहता है। यह clause के main verb को control करता है।

> **Rule box:** Confusing sentence में subject find करने के लिए interrupting phrases हटाएँ और वह head noun या subject clause locate करें जो verb को control करता है।

Subject इन चीज़ों से hidden हो सकता है:

1. **Prepositional phrases** — of the products, in the room, near the gate  
2. **Relative clauses** — who owns the shops, that you bought yesterday  
3. **Long modifiers** — sitting near the window, written by the committee  
4. **Inverted order** — There are many reasons; In the room were three students  
5. **Noun clauses** — What he said was true

Goal first noun find करना नहीं है। Goal वह noun या clause find करना है जिसके बारे में main verb actually बात कर रहा है।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Subject + Prepositional Phrase + Verb

Prepositional phrases अक्सर subject और verb के बीच बैठ जाते हैं।

> The quality **of the products** **is** poor.

Phrase **of the products** सिर्फ **quality** को describe करता है। यह subject का number change नहीं करता।

| Full subject area | Head subject | Correct verb |
|---|---|---|
| The quality of the products | quality | **is** |
| The color of the walls | color | **is** |
| The students in the classroom | students | **are** |
| The price of these books | price | **is** |

### 2. Subject + Relative Clause + Verb

Relative clause sentence को interrupt कर सकता है।

> The man **who owns the shops** **is** here.

Relative clause के अंदर **owns** **man** से agree करता है। लेकिन full sentence का main verb **is** है, और वह भी **man** से agree करता है।

### 3. There + Be Sentences

**There is/there are** sentences में **there** real subject नहीं होता। Real subject usually verb के बाद आता है।

> There **are** many reasons.  
> There **is** one reason.

**There** को singular subject मत मानिए। Verb के बाद देखें।

### 4. Inverted Sentences

कभी-कभी sentence place phrase या negative expression से शुरू होता है, और subject verb के बाद आता है।

> In the room **were** three students.  
> On the wall **hangs** a large painting.

First sentence का subject **three students** है। Second का subject **a large painting** है।

### 5. Clause as Subject

पूरा clause भी subject हो सकता है।

> What he said **was** true.

यहाँ **what he said** one idea की तरह काम कर रहा है। इसलिए **was** correct है।

## Decision Checklist for Finding the Subject

यह step-by-step method use करें:

1. **Main verb या verb blank find करें।** किस verb को agreement चाहिए?  
2. **Left और right दोनों देखें।** Normal order में subject verb से पहले आता है; inverted sentence में बाद में भी आ सकता है।  
3. **Prepositional phrases remove करें।** **of**, **in**, **on**, **with**, **for**, **near**, **among** आदि से शुरू phrases temporarily cross out करें।  
4. **Interrupting relative clauses remove करें।** **who**, **which**, **that**, या **where** से शुरू clauses थोड़ी देर ignore करें।  
5. **Head noun या full subject clause find करें।** यही verb को control करता है।  
6. **Singular या plural check करें।** फिर verb form choose करें।

### Sample 1

> The quality of the products are poor.

Phrase **of the products** हटाएँ।

> The quality ___ poor.

Subject: **quality**. Verb: **is**.

> The quality of the products **is** poor.

### Sample 2

> The man who owns the shops are here.

Relative clause: **who owns the shops**.  
Main subject: **The man**.  
Verb: **is**.

> The man who owns the shops **is** here.

### Sample 3

> In the room was three students.

यह inverted order है। Subject verb के बाद आता है।

Subject: **three students**.  
Verb: **were**.

> In the room **were** three students.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. The quality of the products **is** poor.  
2. The products in the box **are** damaged.  
3. The price of these books **is** high.  
4. The books on the top shelf **are** old.  
5. The man who owns the shops **is** here.  
6. The shops that belong to the man **are** closed.  
7. There **are** many reasons for the delay.  
8. There **is** one serious problem.  
9. In the room **were** three students.  
10. What she promised **was** difficult to deliver.

Pattern clear है: पहले noise हटाएँ, फिर real subject find करें।

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Prepositional Phrase Trap

> The bouquet of roses ___ beautiful.  
> Correct: The bouquet of roses **is** beautiful.

**Roses** plural है, लेकिन prepositional phrase के अंदर है। Subject **bouquet** है।

### Trap 2: Relative Clause Trap

> The teacher who guides the students ___ strict.  
> Correct: The teacher who guides the students **is** strict.

Relative clause में दूसरा noun **students** है, लेकिन वह main verb को control नहीं करता।

### Trap 3: There Is / There Are Trap

> There is many reasons.  
> Correct: There **are** many reasons.

इस structure में real subject verb के बाद आता है। **Many reasons** plural है।

### Trap 4: Place Expressions के बाद Inversion

> Under the table was two bags.  
> Correct: Under the table **were** two bags.

Sentence location से शुरू है, subject से नहीं।

### Trap 5: Collective या Group Nouns

Collective noun meaning और English variety के हिसाब से singular या plural हो सकता है।

> The team **is** ready.  
> The team **are** arguing among themselves. (British English में common)

Exam grammar में expected style और intended meaning के हिसाब से verb choose करें। Detail के लिए [Collective Nouns Agreement](/hi/posts/collective-nouns-agreement/) देखें।

| There **is** many reasons. | There **are** many reasons. | Real subject **many reasons** है। |

## Quick Practice

Correct verb चुनें या sentence correct करें।

1. The answer to all these questions ___ simple.  
   a) is b) are
2. The boys near the entrance ___ waiting.  
   a) is b) are
3. There ___ several mistakes in this paragraph.  
   a) is b) are
4. On the table ___ a glass of water.  
   a) is b) are
5. Error spotting: The result of the tests are final.
6. Error spotting: The woman who teaches the children are absent.
7. Error spotting: Behind the school was two playgrounds.
8. Fill in the blank: The pages of the old book ___ torn.
9. Rewrite correctly: There is three solutions to this problem.
10. Identify the subject: What he wrote was powerful.

### Answer Key

1. **is** — subject **answer** है।  
2. **are** — subject **boys** है।  
3. **are** — real subject **several mistakes** है।  
4. **is** — subject **a glass of water** है।  
5. **is final** — subject **result** है।  
6. **is absent** — subject **woman** है।  
7. **were two playgrounds** — inverted subject plural है।  
8. **are** — subject **pages** है।  
9. **There are three solutions to this problem.**  
10. Subject: **What he wrote**.

## Summary

> **Final rule:** Nearest noun देखकर verb मत चुनें। Extra phrases और clauses हटाएँ, head subject find करें, फिर verb को उसी subject से match करें।

Memory trick: **Foundation judge करने से पहले decoration cross out करो।**

ये examples revise करें:

- The quality of the products **is** poor.  
- There **are** many reasons.  
- In the room **were** three students.
