## Introduction: Prepositions इतने Unpredictable क्यों लगते हैं

Preposition errors अक्सर natural लगते हैं क्योंकि learners अपनी first language से direct translation कर देते हैं।

> Wrong: We discussed **about** the issue.  
> Right: We discussed **the issue**.

Sentence harmless लगता है क्योंकि **about** speaking verbs के बाद अक्सर आता है: **talk about**, **think about**, **argue about**. लेकिन standard English में **discuss** already transitive verb है। यह direct object लेता है, **about + object** नहीं।

इसीलिए preposition error spotting केवल meaning से solve नहीं होती। आपको common patterns भी जानने होते हैं: verb + object, adjective + preposition, noun + preposition, और time/place expressions. **in**, **on**, और **at** की foundation के लिए [In vs On vs At](/hi/posts/in-on-at-prepositions/) revise करें।

## Core Meaning and Rule

Preposition किसी noun या pronoun को sentence के दूसरे part से connect करता है। यह time, place, direction, cause, method, relationship या association दिखा सकता है।

> **Rule box:** Common verb/adjective/noun + preposition patterns सीखें; prepositions को word-for-word translate न करें।

Preposition errors usually तीन sources से आते हैं:

1. **Collocation** — कुछ words naturally कुछ prepositions लेते हैं।  
   - good **at**, interested **in**, responsible **for**
2. **Time and place patterns** — especially **in**, **on**, और **at**.  
   - **on** Monday morning, **in** the morning, **at** 5 pm
3. **Unnecessary prepositions** — कुछ verbs को preposition नहीं चाहिए।  
   - discuss the issue, enter the room, reach the station

Exams में wrong option अक्सर conversational लगता है, लेकिन standard grammar fixed pattern expect करती है।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Verb + Object, Without Extra Preposition

कुछ verbs direct object लेते हैं। उनके बाद extra preposition न लगाएँ।

| Wrong | Correct |
|---|---|
| discuss **about** the matter | discuss **the matter** |
| enter **into** the room | enter **the room** |
| reach **at** the station | reach **the station** |
| order **for** food | order **food** |
| request **for** help | request **help** / make a request for help |

ध्यान रखें: noun form preposition ले सकता है, भले ही verb न ले।

> We **discussed the plan**.  
> We had **a discussion about the plan**.

### 2. Adjective + Preposition Patterns

कई adjective-preposition combinations को units की तरह सीखना पड़ता है।

| Adjective | Common Preposition | Example |
|---|---|---|
| good | at | good **at** English |
| interested | in | interested **in** music |
| afraid | of | afraid **of** spiders |
| responsible | for | responsible **for** the mistake |
| different | from | different **from** mine |
| married | to | married **to** her |
| aware | of | aware **of** the risk |
| famous | for | famous **for** its temples |

### 3. Time: In, On, At

| Preposition | Time Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **at** | exact time | **at** 5 pm, **at** noon |
| **on** | days and dates | **on** Monday, **on** 17 May |
| **in** | months, years, long periods | **in** May, **in** 2026, **in** the morning |

Useful contrast:

> **on Monday morning**  
> **in the morning**  
> **at 5 pm**

Time expression जितना exact होगा, **at** की possibility उतनी बढ़ती है। Named day के साथ **on** आता है।

### 4. Place and Direction

| Meaning | Common Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| inside/enclosed | in | in the room |
| surface | on | on the table |
| exact point | at | at the gate |
| movement toward | to | go to school |
| source/origin | from | come from Delhi |

**In** और **into** confuse न करें। **In** अक्सर position दिखाता है; **into** outside से inside movement दिखाता है।

> She is **in** the room.  
> She walked **into** the room.

## Decision Checklist for Preposition Error Spotting

यह repeatable method use करें:

1. **Preposition से पहले वाला word find करें।** वह verb, adjective, noun या time/place expression है?  
2. **Check करें कि उस word का fixed preposition है या नहीं।**  
3. **पूछें कि verb को preposition नहीं, direct object चाहिए क्या।**  
4. **Time/place के लिए broad-to-specific pattern apply करें।**  
5. **पूरे sentence को meaning और register के लिए पढ़ें।**

### Sample 1

> The teacher discussed **about** the result.

Key word **discussed** है। Verb **discuss** direct object लेता है।

> The teacher discussed **the result**.

### Sample 2

> He is married **with** a doctor.

Adjective/participle **married** spouse name करने पर usually **to** लेता है।

> He is married **to** a doctor.

### Sample 3

> She is good **in** English.

Skill या ability के meaning में common pattern **good at** है।

> She is good **at** English.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. We discussed **the issue** for an hour.  
2. He is married **to her**, not married **with her**.  
3. She is good **at English**.  
4. The meeting starts **at 5 pm**.  
5. I will call you **on Monday morning**.  
6. I usually study **in the morning**.  
7. They reached **the station** before sunset.  
8. She entered **the room** quietly.  
9. He is interested **in history**.  
10. This rule is different **from** the old rule.

Notice करें कि कई corrections में preposition replace नहीं होता; preposition remove होता है।

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Prepositions का Direct Translation

Prepositions अलग-अलग languages में perfectly match नहीं करते। जो phrase Hindi या किसी और language में logical लगता है, वह English में standard न हो सकता है।

Wrong: He is senior **than** me.  
Right: He is senior **to** me.

Wrong: She is angry **on** me.  
Right: She is angry **with** me.

### Trap 2: Similar Verbs, Different Patterns

| Verb | Pattern |
|---|---|
| discuss | discuss the problem |
| talk | talk about the problem |
| reach | reach the station |
| arrive | arrive at the station / arrive in Delhi |
| enter | enter the room |
| go | go into the room / go to school |

Exams अक्सर familiar preposition को wrong verb के बाद रख देते हैं।

### Trap 3: Good At, Good In, Good For

ये तीनों interchangeable नहीं हैं।

> She is good **at** English. (skill)  
> There is a good result **in** the report. (location/context)  
> Exercise is good **for** health. (benefit)

### Trap 4: Married To, Not Married With

Spouse का नाम बताते समय **married to** use करें।

> She is married **to** an engineer.

लेकिन **with** दूसरे meanings में आ सकता है:

> She lives **with** her husband.

### Trap 5: On, In, At with Time

Wrong: I will meet you **in Monday**.  
Right: I will meet you **on Monday**.

Wrong: The class starts **on 9 am**.  
Right: The class starts **at 9 am**.

Wrong: I wake up early **on the morning**.  
Right: I wake up early **in the morning**.

### Trap 6: Unnecessary Prepositions

Wrong: He demanded **for** an explanation.  
Right: He demanded **an explanation**.

Wrong: She resembles **with** her mother.  
Right: She resembles **her mother**.

कई error-spotting questions में wrong preposition extra baggage होता है।

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Discuss **about** the issue. | Discuss **the issue**. | **Discuss** direct object लेता है। |
| Married **with** her. | Married **to** her. | Standard spouse pattern **married to** है। |
| Good **in** English. | Good **at** English. | Skill के लिए **good at**. |
| I will meet you **in Monday morning**. | I will meet you **on Monday morning**. | Named day **on** लेता है। |
| The class starts **on 5 pm**. | The class starts **at 5 pm**. | Exact time **at** लेता है। |
| She entered **into** the room. | She entered **the room**. | **Enter** usually direct object लेता है। |
| He reached **at** the airport. | He reached **the airport**. | **Reach** direct object लेता है। |
| She is interested **for** music. | She is interested **in** music. | Fixed pattern: **interested in**. |

## Quick Practice

### A. Choose the Correct Option

1. She is good ___ mathematics.  
   a) in  b) at  c) for  d) with

2. The train arrived ___ the platform at 6 pm.  
   a) in  b) on  c) at  d) to

3. We discussed ___ the proposal.  
   a) about  b) on  c) of  d) no preposition

4. He is married ___ a journalist.  
   a) with  b) to  c) by  d) for

### B. Spot the Error

5. She entered into the classroom quietly.  
6. I will meet you in Monday evening.  
7. He is responsible for the accounts.

### C. Rewrite Correctly

8. They reached at the station late.  
9. She is interested for classical music.  
10. The class begins on 8 am.

### Answer Key

1. **at** — skill pattern: **good at**.  
2. **at** — platform को specific point की तरह लिया गया है।  
3. **no preposition** — **discuss** direct object लेता है।  
4. **to** — spouse pattern: **married to**.  
5. **entered into → entered** — **enter** usually direct object लेता है।  
6. **in Monday evening → on Monday evening** — named day **on** लेता है।  
7. No error — **responsible for** correct है।  
8. They reached **the station** late.  
9. She is interested **in** classical music.  
10. The class begins **at 8 am**.

## Summary

Preposition error spotting एक grand rule याद करने से नहीं, common patterns पहचानने से आसान होती है।

> **Final rule box:**  
> Preposition से पहले वाला word check करें।  
> Fixed verb/adjective/noun patterns सीखें।  
> Direct object लेने वाले verbs के बाद extra preposition न लगाएँ।  
> Time और place के लिए broad-to-specific logic use करें।

Memory trick: **Prepositions words के साथ travel करते हैं।** **good at**, **interested in**, **married to**, **responsible for**, और **discuss the issue** जैसे phrases को complete units की तरह सीखें।

Exam से पहले ये correct forms revise करें:

> We discussed **the issue**.  
> She is married **to** him.  
> He is good **at** English.  
> I will meet you **on Monday morning**, **in the morning**, **at 5 pm**.
