## Introduction: "Reading, Swimming, and to Dance" Uneven क्यों लगता है?

Parallelism वह grammar rule है जो balanced lists और clean sentences के पीछे काम करता है।

> Weak: She likes **reading**, **swimming**, and **to dance**.  
> Better: She likes **reading**, **swimming**, and **dancing**.

पहला sentence समझ में आ जाता है, लेकिन uneven लगता है क्योंकि list में दो **-ing** forms हैं और एक **to + verb** form है। Ideas equal हैं, लेकिन उनकी grammar match नहीं कर रही।

Parallelism important है क्योंकि English readers similar ideas को similar forms में expect करते हैं। जब list, comparison या paired structure parallel नहीं होता, sentence clumsy, unclear या grammatically weak लग सकता है।

यह topic error spotting, sentence improvement, editing और essay writing में common है। यह सिर्फ exam trick नहीं है; strong writing का basic rule है।

## Core Meaning and Rule

**Parallelism** का मतलब है कि equivalent ideas matching grammatical forms में होने चाहिए।

> **Rule box:** Equal ideas को equal forms में रखें: noun with noun, verb with verb, adjective with adjective, phrase with phrase, clause with clause.

Compare करें:

> Weak: The job requires **skill**, **patience**, and **being careful**.  
> Better: The job requires **skill**, **patience**, and **care**.

Better sentence में तीनों items nouns हैं। Sentence smoother और clearer हो जाता है।

Parallelism अक्सर इन जगहों पर आता है:

- **and** या **or** वाली lists
- paired structures जैसे **either...or**, **neither...nor**, **not only...but also**
- **than** या **as...as** वाली comparisons
- formal writing में repeated phrases
- bullet points, headings, instructions

Rule simple है, लेकिन application subtle हो सकता है। हमेशा पूछें: **कौन-से ideas equal हैं, और क्या उनके forms match कर रहे हैं?**

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### Parallel Lists

| Uneven Form | Parallel Form | Pattern |
|---|---|---|
| reading, swimming, and to dance | **reading, swimming, and dancing** | gerund + gerund + gerund |
| honest, intelligent, and works hard | **honest, intelligent, and hardworking** | adjective + adjective + adjective |
| skill, patience, and being careful | **skill, patience, and care** | noun + noun + noun |
| to plan, writing, and review | **to plan, to write, and to review** | infinitive + infinitive + infinitive |

List में different forms हो सकते हैं, लेकिन जो items equal function कर रहे हैं, उन्हें match करना चाहिए।

### Parallel Verbs

जब subject कई actions करता है, verbs matching form में रखें:

> Better: She **opened** the file, **checked** the data, and **sent** the report.  
> Weak: She **opened** the file, **checked** the data, and **was sending** the report.

Weak version tense/aspect change करता है without reason। अगर time relationship सच में different है, change correct हो सकता है। लेकिन equal actions हों तो forms parallel रखें।

### Parallel Adjectives

जब कई words same noun या subject को describe करें, grammatical category match होनी चाहिए:

> Weak: He is **honest**, **intelligent**, and **works hard**.  
> Better: He is **honest**, **intelligent**, and **hardworking**.

पहले दो items adjectives हैं; तीसरा verb phrase है। Better version में तीनों adjectives हैं।

### Parallel Correlative Conjunctions

Correlative conjunctions में balance especially important है:

> Weak: Either **study hard** or **you will fail**.  
> Better: Either **study hard** or **accept failure**.

Better sentence verb phrase with verb phrase balance करता है।

Other pairs:

> neither **rich** nor **famous**  
> not only **accurate** but also **clear**  
> both **in the classroom** and **at home**

Pair के दोनों halves के बाद आने वाले words similar grammatical shape में होने चाहिए।

### Parallel Comparisons

Comparisons में matching things compare करें:

> Weak: Her explanation is clearer than **the teacher**.  
> Better: Her explanation is clearer than **the teacher's explanation**.  
> Also better: Her explanation is clearer than **the teacher's**.

Weak sentence explanation को person से compare करता हुआ लगता है। Parallelism comparison को logical बनाता है।

## Decision Checklist

Parallelism errors spot करने के लिए यह method use करें:

1. List, pair या comparison find करें.
2. Equal items identify करें.
3. उनके forms label करें: noun, adjective, verb phrase, infinitive, gerund, clause आदि.
4. Matching items को matching forms में बदलें.
5. Sentence को mentally read करें और rhythm check करें.
6. Meaning change न हो, यह confirm करें.

### Checklist Example 1

> She likes reading, swimming, and to dance.

Equal items activities हैं after **likes**. एक form choose करें:

> She likes **reading, swimming, and dancing**.

### Checklist Example 2

> He is honest, intelligent, and works hard.

पहले दो items adjectives हैं। तीसरे को भी adjective-like बनाएं:

> He is **honest, intelligent, and hardworking**.

### Checklist Example 3

> The job requires skill, patience, and being careful.

पहले दो items nouns हैं। तीसरे को भी noun बनाएं:

> The job requires **skill, patience, and care**.

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Gerunds और Infinitives Mix करना

> Weak: She likes **reading**, **swimming**, and **to dance**.  
> Better: She likes **reading**, **swimming**, and **dancing**.  
> Also possible: She likes **to read**, **to swim**, and **to dance**.

दोनों corrected versions parallel हैं। Context के हिसाब से natural option choose करें।

### Trap 2: Adjectives और Verb Phrases Mix करना

> Weak: He is **honest**, **intelligent**, and **works hard**.  
> Better: He is **honest**, **intelligent**, and **hardworking**.

**Is** के बाद sentence subject को describe करने वाले complements expect करता है। Three adjectives better fit करते हैं।

### Trap 3: Unbalanced Correlative Conjunctions

> Weak: Either **study hard** or **you will fail**.  
> Better: Either **study hard** or **accept failure**.  
> Also better: **Either you study hard, or you will fail.**

Second corrected version clause with clause balance करता है। First verb phrase with verb phrase balance करता है।

### Trap 4: Noun + Noun + Gerund Phrase

> Weak: The job requires **skill**, **patience**, and **being careful**.  
> Better: The job requires **skill**, **patience**, and **care**.

Abstract nouns की list suddenly phrase में shift नहीं होनी चाहिए unless there is a reason.

### Trap 5: Faulty Comparisons

> Weak: My marks are higher than **Rahul**.  
> Better: My marks are higher than **Rahul's marks**.  
> Better: My marks are higher than **Rahul's**.

Marks को marks से compare करें, person से नहीं।

### Non-Parallel Form कब Allowed हो सकता है?

हर list mechanically identical नहीं होती। कभी writer meaning के लिए structure change करता है:

> She came to apologize, not to argue.

यह parallel है। लेकिन अगर sentence deliberately two different grammatical ideas contrast करता है, तो strict matching break हो सकता है। Formal grammar questions में assume करें कि equal ideas match होने चाहिए unless context clearly अलग meaning दे रहा हो।

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Better Form | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| She likes reading, swimming, and **to dance**. | She likes **reading, swimming, and dancing**. | Gerund with gerund match करें. |
| He is honest, intelligent, and **works hard**. | He is **honest, intelligent, and hardworking**. | Adjective with adjective match करें. |
| Either **study hard** or **you will fail**. | Either **study hard** or **accept failure**. | Verb phrase with verb phrase. |
| The job requires skill, patience, and **being careful**. | The job requires **skill, patience, and care**. | Noun with noun match करें. |
| Her speech was clear, powerful, and **it inspired us**. | Her speech was **clear, powerful, and inspiring**. | Complement forms match करें. |
| My salary is lower than **my friend**. | My salary is lower than **my friend's salary**. | Salary को salary से compare करें. |

## Quick Practice

Parallelism error correct करें या better option choose करें।

1. She enjoys singing, painting, and to travel.
2. The teacher asked us to read the chapter, write notes, and revising the answers.
3. He is polite, careful, and speaks clearly.
4. Either submit the form or you must pay a fine.
5. The work needs time, effort, and being patient.
6. My result is better than Riya.
7. Choose the parallel form: The course is useful, affordable, and ___. *(easy to follow / it helps students)*
8. Fill in the blank: She promised to call, to explain, and ___ the issue. *(to solve / solving)*
9. Rewrite: The plan is simple, practical, and it saves money.
10. Rewrite: Not only did he lose the file but also the report was deleted.

### Answer Key

1. She enjoys **singing, painting, and traveling**.
2. The teacher asked us **to read** the chapter, **write** notes, and **revise** the answers.
3. He is **polite, careful, and clear-spoken** / **polite, careful, and articulate**.
4. **Either submit the form or pay a fine** / **Either you submit the form, or you must pay a fine**.
5. The work needs **time, effort, and patience**.
6. My result is better than **Riya's**.
7. **easy to follow** — adjective-like list से match करता है.
8. **to solve** — **to call** और **to explain** से match करता है.
9. The plan is **simple, practical, and economical** / **simple, practical, and money-saving**.
10. Not only did he **lose the file**, but he also **deleted the report**.
