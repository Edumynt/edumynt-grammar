## परिचय: एक Modal Real Future के लिए, दूसरा Distance के लिए

कई learners **will** और **would** को सिर्फ same word के present और past forms मान लेते हैं। यह partly true है, लेकिन पूरा rule नहीं है।

> गलत: When I was a child, I **will** play outside every evening.  
> सही: When I was a child, I **would** play outside every evening.

यहाँ "would" future नहीं बता रहा। यह repeated past habit बता रहा है। किसी दूसरे sentence में यह politeness दिखा सकता है:

> **Would** you like some tea?

और conditional sentence में यह imagined result दिखा सकता है:

> If I knew the answer, I **would** tell you.

इसलिए real difference सिर्फ time का नहीं है। **Will** usually real future, willingness, promise, prediction और present decision से जुड़ा है। **Would** distance बनाता है: past distance, polite distance, या imaginary distance. यह idea समझ आते ही ज़्यादातर errors आसान हो जाते हैं।

---

## Core Meaning और Rule

- **Will** = future action, prediction, promise, willingness, instant decision.
- **Would** = past habit, polite request/offer, hypothetical result, future seen from a past point.

> **Rule box:** Real future या present willingness के लिए **will** use करें। Past, polite, imagined, या reported future meaning के लिए **would** use करें।

Compare करें:

> I **will** help you.  
> I **would** help you if I could.

पहला sentence real और direct लगता है। दूसरा sentence imagined condition पर depend करता है।

## Forms, Patterns और Sentence Position

"Will" और "would" modal verbs हैं। इनके बाद main verb का base form आता है।

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| subject + will/would + base verb | She **will come**. / She **would come** if invited. |
| subject + will/would + not + base verb | He **will not agree**. / He **would not agree**. |
| will/would + subject + base verb | **Will** you help me? / **Would** you help me? |
| will/would + have + past participle | They **will have left**. / They **would have left** earlier. |

Modal के बाद -s नहीं लगाते:

> सही: He **will go**.  
> गलत: ~~He will goes.~~

### Main Uses

| Use | Better Modal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Real future | will | I **will call** you tomorrow. |
| Prediction | will | It **will rain** tonight. |
| Promise or willingness | will | I **will help** you. |
| Polite request | would | **Would** you open the window? |
| Polite offer/invitation | would | **Would** you like tea? |
| Past habit | would | We **would play** cricket after school. |
| Hypothetical result | would | If I knew, I **would tell** you. |
| Future in the past | would | He said he **would come**. |

## Decision Checklist

Choose करने से पहले यह method use करें:

1. Sentence real future action या present decision के बारे में है? **Will**.
2. Promise, willingness, या prediction है? Usually **will**.
3. Polite request, invitation, या offer है? **Would** prefer करें।
4. "If" के साथ unreal condition है? Result clause में **would** use करें।
5. Past point से future action report हो रहा है? **Would**.
6. Past में repeated action describe हो रहा है? **Would** work कर सकता है, especially actions के लिए, states के लिए नहीं।

### Checklist Examples

> If I knew the answer, I ___ tell you.

Condition unreal है: "If I knew." Correct: I **would** tell you.

> I ___ send the file tonight.

यह real future promise या decision है। Correct: I **will** send the file tonight.

> He said he ___ meet us at the station.

Reporting verb "said" past में है। Correct: He said he **would** meet us at the station.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. I **will finish** the work today.  
   Real future plan या promise.

2. I **would finish** the work today if I had time.  
   Imagined result.

3. **Will** you attend the meeting?  
   Future action के बारे में direct question.

4. **Would** you attend the meeting if it were online?  
   Hypothetical question.

5. **Would** you like tea?  
   Polite offer. "Will you like tea?" tea offer करने के लिए natural नहीं लगता।

6. When I was young, I **would play** outside every evening.  
   Repeated past habit.

7. She said she **would call** later.  
   Reported speech में future-in-the-past.

8. If he apologises, I **will forgive** him.  
   Real possible condition.

9. If he apologised, I **would forgive** him.  
   Less real या imagined condition.

10. The door **will not open**.  
    इसका meaning refusal या failure हो सकता है: कोशिश के बावजूद door open नहीं हो रहा।

## Conditional Trap: If के बाद Will vs Would

कई exam errors real और unreal conditions mix करने से आते हैं।

| Condition Type | Structure | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Real possible condition | if + present, will + base verb | If I **know**, I **will tell** you. |
| Unreal present condition | if + past, would + base verb | If I **knew**, I **would tell** you. |
| Unreal past condition | if + past perfect, would have + past participle | If I **had known**, I **would have told** you. |

"If" के बाद verb clue देता है। अगर condition में "knew," "had," "were," या कोई past-looking form unreal meaning के साथ है, result में usually **would** चाहिए, **will** नहीं।

> गलत: If I knew, I **will** tell you.  
> सही: If I knew, I **would** tell you.

लेकिन हर "if" के बाद "will" avoid मत करें। Real conditions में main clause में "will" सही है:

> If I get time, I **will** call you.

## Past Habit: Would हमेशा Used To जैसा नहीं होता

"Would" repeated past actions describe कर सकता है:

> During summer vacations, we **would visit** our grandparents.

लेकिन past states के साथ यह कमजोर होता है। States के लिए **used to** usually better है।

> बेहतर: I **used to have** long hair.  
> Awkward: I **would have** long hair.

Past habit के लिए "would" usually clear past-time setting मांगता है, जैसे "when I was a child," "in those days," या "during college." Setting न हो तो reader इसे hypothetical समझ सकता है।

## Politeness और Register

"Would" request को softer बनाता है क्योंकि यह social distance create करता है।

> **Will** you close the door?  
> **Would** you close the door?

दोनों correct हो सकते हैं, लेकिन दूसरा more polite लगता है। Formal communication, customer service, interviews और emails में "would" अक्सर safer है:

> **Would** you please confirm your availability?  
> **Would** it be possible to reschedule the meeting?

"Will" अपने आप rude नहीं है। यह बस more direct है।

## Exam Traps और Usage Notes

### 1. Reported Speech

Past reporting verb के बाद future sentence report करते समय "will" अक्सर "would" बनता है।

> Direct: He said, "I **will** come."  
> Reported: He said he **would** come.

अगर future event अभी भी certain या official है, कुछ speakers "will" रख सकते हैं, लेकिन exams usually backshift expect करते हैं: **will → would**.

### 2. Polite Offers

> सही: **Would** you like some coffee?  
> Unnatural: ~~Will you like some coffee?~~

"Will you like" future preference पूछता है, normal offer नहीं।

### 3. Past Habit vs Future Meaning

> गलत: When we were children, we **will** swim in the river.  
> सही: When we were children, we **would** swim in the river.

Time marker "when we were children" real future meaning को block करता है।

### 4. Would Have for Missed Possibilities

Unreal past results के लिए **would have + past participle** use करें।

> If she had studied harder, she **would have passed**.

"Would passed" या "would have pass" मत लिखें।

## Common Mistakes

| गलत | सही | क्यों |
|---|---|---|
| If I know, I **would** tell you. | If I know, I **will** tell you. | Real condition में result के लिए will. |
| If I knew, I **will** tell you. | If I knew, I **would** tell you. | Unreal condition में would. |
| **Will** you like tea? | **Would** you like tea? | Polite offer. |
| When I was a child, I **will** play outside. | When I was a child, I **would** play outside. | Past habit. |
| He said he **will** come. | He said he **would** come. | Past point से reported future. |
| She would **goes** there. | She would **go** there. | Modal + base verb. |

## Quick Practice

Correct option चुनें।

1. If I see him, I ___ tell him.  
   a) will  b) would

2. If I saw him, I ___ tell him.  
   a) will  b) would

3. ___ you like some water?  
   a) Will  b) Would

4. He promised he ___ return soon.  
   a) will  b) would

Error find और correct करें।

5. When she was in school, she will read two books a week.

6. If I had enough money, I will buy a house.

7. He said he will meet us after lunch.

Rewrite या fill in the blank.

8. Politely rewrite करें: "Will you send me the details?"

9. Fill in: If they had left earlier, they ___ have reached on time.

10. Fill in: I ___ help you tomorrow. Do not worry.

### Answer Key

1. **will** — real possible condition.  
2. **would** — unreal condition.  
3. **Would** — polite offer.  
4. **would** — past point से reported promise.  
5. When she was in school, she **would read** two books a week.  
6. If I had enough money, I **would buy** a house.  
7. He said he **would meet** us after lunch.  
8. **Would** you send me the details?  
9. **would** — unreal past result: would have reached.  
10. **will** — real future promise.

## Summary

> **Final rule:** **Will** direct और real है: future, promise, prediction, willingness. **Would** distant है: past habit, politeness, reported future, और imagined result.

Memory trick: **will = real**, **would = remote**.

ये pairs revise करें:

> I **will** tell you if I know.  
> I **would** tell you if I knew.  
> He said he **would** come.  
> **Would** you like tea?
