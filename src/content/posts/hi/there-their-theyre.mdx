## तीन शब्द, एक आवाज़, अलग अर्थ

### "They're" — They Are (वे हैं)

Contraction: "They're coming" = "They are coming."

Test: "they are" से replace करें।

### "Their" — Possession (उनका)

Ownership: "Their house" (उनका घर), "their idea" (उनका ख़याल)।

Test: क्या ownership दर्शाता है?

### "There" — जगह या Existential

जगह: "Put it there" (वहाँ रखो), "over there" (उधर)।

Existential: "There is a problem" (एक समस्या है), "There are many options" (कई विकल्प हैं)।

---

## Memory Trick

- **They're** → apostrophe → contraction → "they are"
- **Their** → "heir" अंदर है → inheritance → possession
- **There** → "here" अंदर है → दोनों जगह की बात है

---

## आम गलतियाँ

| गलत | सही |
|---|---|
| **Their** coming soon. | **They're** coming soon. |
| **There** house is big. | **Their** house is big. |
| **They're** is a problem. | **There** is a problem. |

## अभ्यास प्रश्न

1. _____ going to the market.
2. _____ are many reasons to study hard.
3. The children love _____ new school.
4. Put the bag over _____.
5. I think _____ right.

### उत्तर कुंजी

1. **They're** (= They are)
2. **There** (existential)
3. **their** (possessive)
4. **there** (जगह)
5. **they're** (= they are)

---

## सारांश

- **They're** = they are (contraction)
- **Their** = उनका (possession)
- **There** = जगह या "there is/are"
