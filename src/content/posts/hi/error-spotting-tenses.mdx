## Introduction: Tense Errors Usually Logic Errors होते हैं

Tense mistakes हमेशा बहुत obvious नहीं होते। Sentence लगभग correct लग सकता है, फिर भी उसका time frame गलत हो सकता है।

> Wrong: I have seen him **yesterday**.  
> Right: I **saw** him yesterday.

Word **yesterday** finished past time दिखाता है, इसलिए present perfect **have seen** fit नहीं होता। Correct choice simple past है: **saw**.

Error spotting में tense questions usually all twelve tense names recite करने के लिए नहीं होते। वे check करते हैं कि आप verb form को time markers, action sequence और verb meaning से connect कर पा रहे हैं या नहीं। Nearby time expressions के लिए [Since vs For](/hi/posts/since-vs-for/) और [Used To vs Be Used To](/hi/posts/used-to-vs-be-used-to/) revise करें।

## Core Meaning and Rule

Tense दिखाता है कि action या state time से कैसे जुड़ा है: present, past, future, completion, continuity, habit या sequence.

> **Rule box:** Tense को time marker और sentence logic से match करें, सिर्फ nearby words से नहीं।

Tense error spotting तीन questions पर depend करती है:

1. **Sentence किस time को refer कर रहा है?**  
   - now, yesterday, since 2020, by the time, tomorrow
2. **Action किस type का है?**  
   - habitual, ongoing, completed, repeated, temporary, state
3. **Sequence क्या है?**  
   - कौन-सा action पहले हुआ, बाद में हुआ, या अभी भी continue हो रहा है?

जब ये तीन बातें clear हो जाती हैं, tense errors दिखने लगते हैं।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Finished Past Time: Simple Past

Finished past time expressions के साथ simple past use करें।

| Time Marker | Correct Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| yesterday | simple past | I **met** him yesterday. |
| last week | simple past | She **visited** Delhi last week. |
| in 2019 | simple past | They **moved** here in 2019. |
| two days ago | simple past | He **called** two days ago. |

Finished past time marker के साथ present perfect use न करें।

> Wrong: I **have completed** the work yesterday.  
> Right: I **completed** the work yesterday.

### 2. Since/For + Continuing Situation: Present Perfect

जब action past में शुरू हुआ और अब भी continue है, present perfect या present perfect continuous use करें।

| Meaning | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| state continuing until now | has/have + past participle | I **have lived** here since 2020. |
| activity continuing until now | has/have been + -ing | She **has been working** since morning. |

Time expression **since 2020** past को present से connect करता है। इसलिए **I am living here since 2020** standard नहीं है।

### 3. Stative Verbs: Usually Simple, Not Continuous

कुछ verbs states describe करते हैं, actions नहीं। Ordinary meaning में वे usually continuous form नहीं लेते।

| Stative Verb | Better Form |
|---|---|
| know | She **knows** the answer. |
| believe | I **believe** you. |
| own | He **owns** a car. |
| understand | We **understand** the rule. |
| need | They **need** help. |

इसीलिए standard English में **She is knowing the answer** wrong है।

### 4. Earlier Past: Past Perfect

जब दो past actions हों और एक पहले हुआ हो, sequence clear करने के लिए earlier action के लिए past perfect use होता है।

> When I reached the station, the train **had left**.

Train पहले जा चुकी थी। मैं बाद में पहुँचा। Past perfect order clear करता है।

### 5. Future Time: Present Forms भी Correct हो सकते हैं

English scheduled future events के लिए अक्सर present forms use करती है।

> The train **leaves** at 6 am tomorrow.  
> I **am meeting** her tonight.

इसलिए future time expression देखकर present form को blindly wrong न मानें। Check करें कि यह timetable, arrangement, plan या prediction है। Modal future meanings के लिए [Will vs Would](/hi/posts/will-vs-would/) देखें।

## Decision Checklist for Tense Error Spotting

यह method use करें:

1. **Time marker underline करें।** yesterday, since, for, already, yet, when, before, by the time, now, usually, tomorrow जैसे words देखें।  
2. **Action type identify करें।** Habit, state, ongoing action, completed action, या earlier past?  
3. **Sequence check करें।** अगर दो past actions हैं, पूछें कौन-सा पहले हुआ।  
4. **Stative verbs check करें।** know, believe, own, need, understand जैसे verbs के साथ continuous avoid करें।  
5. **Meaning के लिए sentence पढ़ें।** Tense mechanically correct न करें।

### Sample 1

> I have seen him yesterday.

Time marker: **yesterday**. Finished past time. Correction:

> I **saw** him yesterday.

### Sample 2

> She is knowing the answer.

Verb: **know**, stative verb. Correction:

> She **knows** the answer.

### Sample 3

> When I reached, he left.

अगर meaning यह है कि मेरे पहुँचने से पहले वह जा चुका था, earlier action past perfect में होगा।

> When I reached, he **had left**.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. I **saw him yesterday**, not **have seen him yesterday**.  
2. She **knows the answer**.  
3. When I reached the office, he **had left**.  
4. I **have lived here since 2020**.  
5. They **visited** Mumbai last month.  
6. She **has been studying** for three hours.  
7. He usually **goes** to work by bus.  
8. The train **leaves** at 7 am tomorrow.  
9. I **am reading** a novel these days.  
10. By the time we arrived, the movie **had started**.

Correct tense meaning पर depend करता है, सिर्फ एक isolated word पर नहीं।

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Present Perfect with Finished Past Time

Wrong: I have submitted the form yesterday.  
Right: I submitted the form yesterday.

Present perfect past action को present से connect करता है, लेकिन usually **yesterday** या **last year** जैसे finished past time markers के साथ नहीं आता।

### Trap 2: Since/For with Simple Present or Present Continuous

Wrong: I live here since 2020.  
Better: I have lived here since 2020.

Wrong: She is working here for five years.  
Better: She has worked here for five years. / She has been working here for five years.

**Since** starting point देता है; **for** duration देता है। अगर situation अब भी continue है, तो present perfect common होता है।

### Trap 3: Stative Verbs in Continuous Form

Wrong: I am understanding your problem.  
Right: I understand your problem.

कुछ verbs continuous हो सकते हैं जब meaning change हो:

> I **think** this is right. (opinion)  
> I **am thinking** about your offer. (mental activity)

इसलिए verb alone enough नहीं है; meaning check करें।

### Trap 4: Two Past Actions Without Sequence

Wrong: When we reached the hall, the lecture started.  
Better: When we reached the hall, the lecture **had started**. (अगर lecture पहले शुरू हो चुका था)

अगर actions one after another हुए, simple past correct हो सकता है:

> When we reached the hall, the lecture **started**. (lecture हमारे पहुँचने के बाद शुरू हुआ)

### Trap 5: Habit vs Temporary Action

> He **works** in Delhi. (regular job/state)  
> He **is working** in Delhi this month. (temporary situation)

Error spotting में **usually**, **always**, **every day**, और **often** जैसे words present habits के लिए simple present की ओर point करते हैं।

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct | Why |
|---|---|---|
| I **have seen** him yesterday. | I **saw** him yesterday. | Finished past time simple past लेता है। |
| She **is knowing** the answer. | She **knows** the answer. | **Know** usually stative है। |
| When I reached, he **left**. | When I reached, he **had left**. | Sequence matter करे तो earlier past action past perfect लेता है। |
| I **am living** here since 2020. | I **have lived** here since 2020. | Since past को present से connect करता है। |
| He **go** to school every day. | He **goes** to school every day. | Habit with third-person singular **-s** लेता है। |
| By the time I arrived, she **went**. | By the time I arrived, she **had gone**. | Earlier completed past action. |
| I **did not knew** the answer. | I **did not know** the answer. | **Did** के बाद base verb आता है। |

## Quick Practice

### A. Choose the Correct Option

1. I ___ him yesterday.  
   a) have met  b) met  c) had meet  d) am meeting

2. She ___ here since 2021.  
   a) lives  b) is living  c) has lived  d) lived

3. He ___ the answer.  
   a) is knowing  b) knows  c) know  d) has knowing

4. When I arrived, the bus ___.  
   a) leaves  b) left  c) had left  d) has left

### B. Spot the Error

5. I have finished the report last night.  
6. She has been working here for two years.  
7. He did not went to school yesterday.

### C. Rewrite Correctly

8. I am living in Jaipur since 2018.  
9. She is believing every word he says.  
10. By the time we reached, the match started.

### Answer Key

1. **met** — **yesterday** simple past लेता है।  
2. **has lived** — **since 2021** continuation to now दिखाता है।  
3. **knows** — **know** stative है।  
4. **had left** — bus मेरे पहुँचने से पहले जा चुकी थी।  
5. **have finished → finished** — finished past time: **last night**.  
6. No error — present perfect continuous duration continuing now के लिए सही है।  
7. **did not went → did not go** — **did** के बाद base verb आता है।  
8. I **have lived** in Jaipur since 2018.  
9. She **believes** every word he says.  
10. By the time we reached, the match **had started**.

## Summary

Tense error spotting time logic का test है। Tense सिर्फ इसलिए choose न करें क्योंकि वह formal या complicated लगता है।

> **Final rule box:**  
> Finished past time → simple past.  
> Since/for continuing to now → present perfect.  
> Two past actions में earlier action → past perfect.  
> Stative verb → usually simple, not continuous.  
> Habit → simple tense; temporary action → continuous tense.

Memory trick: **Verb fix करने से पहले time signal find करें।**

ये correct forms revise करें:

> I **saw** him yesterday.  
> She **knows** the answer.  
> He **had left** when I reached.  
> I **have lived** here since 2020.
