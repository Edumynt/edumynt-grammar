## Introduction: जब आप खुद नहीं करते

English में special verb patterns दिखाते हैं कि आप किसी और से कुछ करवाते हैं या service arrange करते हैं।

> I **cut my hair**. *(मैंने खुद किया — unlikely)*  
> I **had my hair cut**. *(किसी और ने काटा।)*

दूसरा sentence **causative** structure use करता है। यह article *have*, *get* और *make* के causative patterns cover करता है।

---

## Core Meaning and Rule

> **Rule box:** Service के लिए **have/get + object + past participle**। Force के लिए **have/make + object + base verb**। Persuade के लिए **get + object + to + verb**।

| Pattern | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| have + object + past participle | service arrange करना | I **had** my car **repaired**. |
| get + object + past participle | service (informal) | I **got** my car **repaired**. |
| make + object + base verb | force करना | She **made** me **wait**. |
| have + object + base verb | instruct करना | I **had** him **fix** it. |
| get + object + to + verb | persuade करना | I **got** him **to fix** it. |

---

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Have + Object + Past Participle (Service)

> I **had my hair cut** yesterday.  
> We **had the house painted** last month.  
> She **had her phone repaired**.

Subject किसी और से action करवाता है। Doer mention नहीं होता।

### 2. Get + Object + Past Participle (Service, Informal)

> I **got my hair cut**.  
> We **got the car serviced**.

*Have* जैसा ही meaning, लेकिन ज़्यादा informal।

### 3. Make + Object + Base Verb (Force)

> She **made me wait** for an hour.  
> The teacher **made** the students **rewrite** the essay.  
> He **made** her **apologise**.

**Make** force या authority imply करता है। Verb से पहले *to* नहीं।

### 4. Have + Object + Base Verb (Instruct)

> I **had the mechanic check** the brakes.  
> She **had her assistant send** the email.

**Have** instructing या requesting imply करता है।

### 5. Get + Object + To + Verb (Persuade)

> I **got him to repair** the bike.  
> She **got her parents to agree**.

**Get** persuasion imply करता है — किसी को convince करना।

---

## Decision Checklist

1. **क्या service है (कोई आपके लिए कुछ करता है)?** → have/get + object + past participle।
2. **क्या किसी को force कर रहे हैं?** → make + object + base verb।
3. **क्या किसी को instruct कर रहे हैं?** → have + object + base verb।
4. **क्या किसी को persuade कर रहे हैं?** → get + object + to + verb।
5. **Form check करें।** *Make* के बाद *to* नहीं। *Get* (persuade) के बाद *to* ज़रूरी।

### Sample 1

> I cut my hair yesterday.

अगर आपका मतलब किसी और ने काटा:

> I **had my hair cut** yesterday.

### Sample 2

> She made me to wait.

**Make** base verb लेता है (no *to*)।

> She made me **wait**.

### Sample 3

> I got him repair it.

**Get** (persuade) को *to* चाहिए।

> I got him **to repair** it.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. I **had my hair cut**. *(service)*
2. I **got my car serviced**. *(service, informal)*
3. She **made me wait**. *(force)*
4. I **had him fix** the tap. *(instruct)*
5. I **got him to fix** the tap. *(persuade)*
6. We **had the house painted**. *(service)*
7. The boss **made** them **work** late. *(force)*
8. I **got the tailor to alter** the shirt. *(persuade)*
9. She **had her nails done**. *(service)*
10. He **made** the children **clean** their rooms. *(force)*

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Make + To + Verb

> Wrong: She made me **to wait**.  
> Right: She made me **wait**.

**Make** bare infinitive (no *to*) लेता है।

### Trap 2: Get + Base Verb (Missing To)

> Wrong: I got him **repair** it.  
> Right: I got him **to repair** it.

**Get** (persuade) के लिए *to + verb* ज़रूरी।

### Trap 3: Have/Get Service में Doer Use करना

> Wrong: I had my hair cut **by the barber**. *(usually unnecessary)*  
> Right: I had my hair cut.

Agent usually omitted होता है causative service sentences में।

### Trap 4: Make और Get को Confuse करना

> Wrong: She **got me wait**.  
> Right: She **made me wait**. *(force)* / She **got me to wait**. *(persuade)*

**Make** = force। **Get** = persuade (requires *to*)।

---

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Right | Why |
|---|---|---|
| She made me **to wait**. | She made me **wait**. | Make + base verb. |
| I got him **repair** it. | I got him **to repair** it. | Get + to + verb. |
| I cut my hair yesterday. | I **had my hair cut** yesterday. | Causative for services. |

## Quick Practice

Correct option चुनें।

1. I ___ my car serviced yesterday.  
   a) had b) made
2. She made me ___ for two hours.  
   a) to wait b) wait
3. I got him ___ the report.  
   a) to finish b) finish
4. We ___ the house painted last month.  
   a) had b) made
5. Error spotting: She made me to apologise.
6. Error spotting: I got him repair the bike.
7. Error spotting: I cut my hair at the salon.
8. Fill in the blank: I had my watch ___. (repair)
9. Rewrite correctly: She made me to rewrite the essay.
10. Choose: I got the mechanic ___ my car. (check / to check)

### Answer Key

1. **had** — service.  
2. **wait** — make + base verb.  
3. **to finish** — get + to + verb.  
4. **had** — service.  
5. **She made me apologise.**  
6. **I got him to repair the bike.**  
7. **I had my hair cut at the salon.**  
8. **repaired** — past participle.  
9. **She made me rewrite the essay.**  
10. **to check** — get + to + verb.

## Summary

> **Rule:** Have/get + object + past participle = service। Make + base verb = force। Get + to + verb = persuade.

Memory trick: **Have it done. Make them do. Get them to do.**

ये revise करें:

- I **had my hair cut**. *(service)*  
- She **made me wait**. *(force)*  
- I **got him to fix** it. *(persuade)*  
- We **had the house painted**. *(service)*
