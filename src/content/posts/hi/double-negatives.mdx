## Introduction: एक Negative Usually काफी होता है

Double negative तब होता है जब एक ही negative meaning के लिए दो negative words use किए जाते हैं।

> Non-standard: I **don't** know **nothing**.  
> Standard: I **don't** know **anything**.  
> Also standard: I know **nothing**.

Intended meaning simple है: speaker को knowledge नहीं है। लेकिन standard English में **don't** already negative है, इसलिए object usually **anything** होता है, **nothing** नहीं।

Double negatives songs, films, informal speech और कुछ dialects में common हैं। इसका मतलब यह नहीं कि वे standard school grammar, formal writing या competitive exams में acceptable हैं। Exams में **I don't know nothing**, **She never said nothing**, और **There isn't no problem** जैसे sentences errors माने जाते हैं।

यह topic [Some vs Any](/hi/posts/some-vs-any/) से naturally connect होता है, क्योंकि negative sentences में अक्सर **any/anything/anyone/anywhere** use होते हैं। Subject, verb और object identify करने के लिए [Basic Sentence Patterns in English](/hi/posts/basic-sentence-patterns/) भी helpful है।

## Core Meaning and Rule

**Negative marker** वह word है जो sentence को negative बनाता है। Common negative markers हैं:

- **not**, **n't**: do not, don't, cannot, can't, isn't, wasn't
- **no**: no answer, no problem, no money
- **never**
- **nothing**, **nobody**, **no one**, **nowhere**
- negative adverbs जैसे **hardly**, **scarcely**, **barely**

Standard English में, अगर आपको एक negative meaning चाहिए, तो usually **one negative marker** use करें।

> **Rule box:** Standard English में एक negative meaning के लिए एक negative marker use करें।

Compare करें:

> Standard: I **don't** know **anything**.  
> Standard: I know **nothing**.  
> Non-standard: I **don't** know **nothing**.

पहला sentence **don't** को negative marker और **anything** को object बनाता है। दूसरा **nothing** को negative marker बनाता है। तीसरा **don't** और **nothing** दोनों use करता है, इसलिए यह double negative बन जाता है।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Not + Any Words

यह सबसे common standard pattern है।

| Meaning | Standard Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| no thing | not + anything | I don't need **anything**. |
| no person | not + anyone/anybody | She didn't meet **anyone**. |
| no place | not + anywhere | We couldn't find it **anywhere**. |
| no amount | not + any | They don't have **any** money. |

Negative verb के बाद **any** forms use करें।

> Correct: There isn't **any** problem.  
> Not standard: There isn't **no** problem.

### 2. No Words Without Not

आप **no**, **nothing**, **nobody**, **no one**, या **nowhere** को another negative verb के बिना भी use कर सकते हैं।

| Standard Sentence | Meaning |
|---|---|
| I know **nothing**. | I do not know anything. |
| **Nobody** came to the meeting. | No person came. |
| We found **nowhere** to sit. | We did not find any place. |
| There is **no** problem. | There is not any problem. |

यह pattern often more direct और sometimes more formal होता है।

### 3. Never + Any Words

**Never** का meaning already **not ever** है। Ordinary negative meaning के लिए इसके बाद **nothing**, **nobody**, या **nowhere** न लगाएँ।

> Non-standard: She **never** said **nothing**.  
> Standard: She **never** said **anything**.

### 4. Hardly, Scarcely, Barely

**Hardly**, **scarcely**, और **barely** जैसे words meaning में negative होते हैं। इनका meaning है **almost not**.

> Wrong: He **can't hardly** speak.  
> Correct: He **can hardly** speak.

क्योंकि **hardly** already negative idea रखता है, **can't** add करना standard grammar में usually incorrect double negative बनाता है।

## Decision Checklist

Error spotting और sentence correction में यह method use करें।

1. सारे negative words identify करें: **not**, **no**, **never**, **nothing**, **nobody**, **hardly**, etc.
2. पूछें sentence को one negative meaning चाहिए या two separate negative ideas।
3. अगर one negative meaning है, तो only one negative marker रखें।
4. Negative verb के बाद **no/nothing/nobody/nowhere** को **any/anything/anyone/anywhere** से replace करें।
5. Sentence दोबारा read करके standard meaning और register check करें।

### Example 1

> I **don't** know **nothing**.

- Negative markers: **don't**, **nothing**
- Intended meaning: one negative idea
- Correct options: I **don't** know **anything**. / I know **nothing**.

### Example 2

> She **never** said **nothing**.

- Negative markers: **never**, **nothing**
- Correct: She **never** said **anything**.

### Example 3

> He **can't** **hardly** speak.

- Negative markers: **can't**, **hardly**
- Correct: He **can hardly** speak.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. Non-standard: I **don't** know **nothing**.  
   Standard: I **don't** know **anything**. / I know **nothing**.

2. Non-standard: She **never** said **nothing**.  
   Standard: She **never** said **anything**.

3. Non-standard: There **isn't** **no** problem.  
   Standard: There **isn't** **any** problem. / There is **no** problem.

4. Non-standard: He **can't** **hardly** speak.  
   Standard: He **can hardly** speak.

5. Non-standard: We **didn't** see **nobody** at the gate.  
   Standard: We **didn't** see **anybody** at the gate. / We saw **nobody** at the gate.

6. Non-standard: They **haven't** received **no** reply.  
   Standard: They **haven't** received **any** reply. / They have received **no** reply.

7. Non-standard: I **can't** find my keys **nowhere**.  
   Standard: I **can't** find my keys **anywhere**. / I can find my keys **nowhere**.

8. Non-standard: The teacher **didn't** give **no** homework.  
   Standard: The teacher **didn't** give **any** homework. / The teacher gave **no** homework.

9. Standard with two real negative ideas: Not all students were unhappy.  
   Meaning: कुछ students happy हो सकते थे।

10. Standard with contrast: I am not saying nothing; I am saying very little.  
    यह special, deliberate logic है, normal exam pattern नहीं।

Examples 9 और 10 important point दिखाते हैं: दो negative-looking words वाला हर sentence automatically wrong नहीं होता। लेकिन school-level double-negative errors में usually one negative meaning दो बार express किया जाता है।

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Don't के बाद Nothing

**don't**, **doesn't**, **didn't**, **isn't**, **wasn't**, **can't** जैसे negatives के बाद **anything/anyone/anywhere/any** use करें, **nothing/nobody/nowhere/no** नहीं।

> Wrong: I don't want **nothing**.  
> Correct: I don't want **anything**.

### Trap 2: Never Already Negative है

**Never** का meaning **not ever** है। Ordinary standard meaning में इसे **nothing** के साथ combine न करें।

> Wrong: He never helps **nobody**.  
> Correct: He never helps **anybody**.

### Trap 3: Hardly Meaning में Negative है

Students अक्सर **hardly** को **slowly** या **clearly** जैसे normal adverb की तरह treat करते हैं, लेकिन **hardly** का meaning है **almost not**.

> Wrong: She couldn't hardly hear me.  
> Correct: She could hardly hear me.

### Trap 4: Informal Speech हमेशा Exam Grammar नहीं होती

कुछ dialects में double negatives emphasis के लिए use होते हैं:

> I don't need no advice.

Informal speech में यह understandable है, लेकिन formal English में non-standard है। School grammar और competitive exams में इसे correct करें:

> I don't need any advice.

### Trap 5: Negative Prefixes अलग चीज़ हैं

**unhappy**, **impossible**, **disagree**, **irregular** जैसे words में negative prefixes होते हैं। ये हमेशा same double-negative error नहीं बनाते।

> Not impossible = possible, या कम से कम possible enough.

यह logical double negative है और deliberate भी हो सकता है। Exam error usually simpler होता है: **not + nothing**, **never + nobody**, **can't + hardly**.

## Summary

Double negatives standard English में usually incorrect होते हैं जब वे केवल one negative meaning express करते हैं।

> **Final rule:** One negative idea के लिए one negative marker use करें: **not + any**, या **no/nothing/nobody** without **not**.

Memory trick: **One negative job, one negative word.**

ये correct pairs revise करें:

> I **don't** know **anything**. / I know **nothing**.  
> She **never** said **anything**.  
> He **can hardly** speak.

Informal speech emphasis के लिए double negatives use कर सकती है, लेकिन standard grammar और exams one-negative rule expect करते हैं।
