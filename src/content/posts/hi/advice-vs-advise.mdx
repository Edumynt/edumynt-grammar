## Rule

- **Advice** = noun (सलाह — एक चीज़). "She gave me good **advice**."
- **Advise** = verb (सलाह देना — एक क्रिया). "I **advise** you to study."

---

## Pronunciation फरक

- **Advice** = /s/ sound (जैसे "ice")
- **Advise** = /z/ sound (जैसे "eyes")

---

## -C / -S Pattern

| Noun (-c) | Verb (-s) |
|---|---|
| advice | advise |
| practice | practise |
| licence | license |
