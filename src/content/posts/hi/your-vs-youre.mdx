## Rule

- **Your** = possessive (तुम्हारा). "Your book," "your idea."
- **You're** = you are (तुम हो). "You're welcome," "you're late."

---

## Substitution Test

"you are" से replace करें:

> **You're** right. = **You are** right. ✓ → **you're**

> **Your** book is here. = **You are** book is here. ✗ → **your**

---

## आम गलतियाँ

| गलत | सही |
|---|---|
| **Your** welcome. | **You're** welcome. |
| **Your** a good student. | **You're** a good student. |
| **You're** phone is ringing. | **Your** phone is ringing. |

---

## अभ्यास प्रश्न

1. _____ going to be late.
2. Is this _____ pen?
3. _____ the best teacher I've ever had.

### उत्तर कुंजी

1. **You're** (= You are)
2. **your** (possessive)
3. **You're** (= You are)

---

## सारांश

- **You're** = you are. **Your** = तुम्हारा (possession).
- Test: "you are" से replace करें। काम करे → you're.
