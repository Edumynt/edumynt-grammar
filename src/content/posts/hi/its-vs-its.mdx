## परिचय: अंग्रेज़ी में सबसे आम Apostrophe गलती

"The dog wagged it's tail." "Its a nice day." यह गलतियाँ हर जगह हैं।

Confusing इसलिए है क्योंकि English में apostrophe + s आमतौर पर possessive दर्शाता है (John's book, the cat's tail)। लेकिन "its" यह rule तोड़ता है — possessive में **कोई apostrophe नहीं** है।

---

## Rule

- **It's** = it is या it has (contraction). "It's raining" = "It is raining."
- **Its** = possessive (इसका). "The dog wagged its tail."

---

## Simple Test

"it is" या "it has" से replace करें:

> **It's** raining. = **It is** raining. ✓ → **it's**

> The dog wagged **its** tail. = The dog wagged **it is** tail. ✗ → **its**

> **It's** been great. = **It has** been great. ✓ → **it's**

> The company changed **its** policy. = The company changed **it is** policy. ✗ → **its**

---

## आम गलतियाँ

| गलत | सही |
|---|---|
| The dog wagged **it's** tail. | The dog wagged **its** tail. |
| **Its** a beautiful day. | **It's** a beautiful day. |
| The tree lost **it's** leaves. | The tree lost **its** leaves. |
