## Introduction: Tense Errors Usually Clue Errors होते हैं

Error spotting में tense questions सिर्फ twelve tense names याद करके solve नहीं होते। इन्हें clues पढ़कर solve किया जाता है: time words, sequence of actions, condition clauses, और action का meaning.

> Wrong: She has gone yesterday.  
> Right: She **went** yesterday.

**Yesterday** finished past time दिखाता है। Standard English में finished past time के साथ simple past use होता है, present perfect नहीं।

Tense errors important हैं क्योंकि वे time, order और logic को affect करते हैं। Vocabulary correct हो सकती है, लेकिन tense clue से match नहीं करता तो sentence wrong हो जाता है।

Related tense work के लिए [Error Spotting Tricks for Tenses](/hi/posts/error-spotting-tenses/) और [Since vs For](/hi/posts/since-vs-for/) revise करें।

## Core Meaning and Rule

Tense action का time दिखाता है। Aspect बताता है कि speaker action को कैसे देख रहा है: complete, continuing, repeated, present से connected, या किसी past action से पहले।

> **Rule box:** Finished past time के साथ **simple past**, present relevance और no finished past time के साथ **present perfect**, और two past actions में earlier action के लिए **past perfect** use करें।

तीन clues usually answer decide करते हैं:

| Clue type | क्या check करें | Common tense choice |
|---|---|---|
| Time adverb | yesterday, last year, already, since, for | past simple या present perfect |
| Sequence | कौन-सा action पहले हुआ? | earlier past के लिए past perfect |
| Clause pattern | when, before, after, if, unless | future main clause के बाद often present simple |

Tense को time और meaning दोनों से match करना चाहिए।

## Forms, Patterns, and Sentence Position

### 1. Did + Base Verb

**Did**, **did not**, या **didn't** के बाद verb की base form आती है।

> Wrong: I did not **met** him.  
> Right: I did not **meet** him.

**Did** पहले ही past tense carry कर रहा है। Main verb को फिर past form में न डालें।

| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| did not went | did not **go** |
| did not saw | did not **see** |
| did not completed | did not **complete** |

### 2. Finished Past Time = Simple Past

Finished time expressions जैसे **yesterday**, **last night**, **in 2019**, **two days ago**, और **when I was young** के साथ simple past use करें।

> She **went** yesterday.  
> I **met** him two days ago.

Standard exam grammar में finished past time marker के साथ present perfect use न करें।

### 3. Present Perfect = Connection to Now

जब action का present relevance हो और finished past time mention न हो, present perfect use करें।

> I **have completed** the work.  
> She **has lost** her keys.

Focus present result पर है: work now complete है; keys now missing हैं।

### 4. Past Perfect = Earlier Past

जब एक past action दूसरे past action से पहले हुआ हो, past perfect use करें।

> By the time I arrived, he **had left**.

Two past actions: पहले वह left हुआ; बाद में I arrived. Earlier action **had + past participle** लेता है।

### 5. Future Main Clause + Present Time Clause

**When**, **before**, **after**, **as soon as**, और **until** के बाद time clauses में future meaning के लिए present simple use होता है।

> Wrong: I will call when I **will reach**.  
> Right: I will call when I **reach**.

Main clause में **will** है। Time clause usually present simple लेता है।

## Decision Checklist for Tense Error Spotting

यह method use करें:

1. **Question वाला verb find करें।**  
2. **Time marker find करें।** Finished past, present relevance, future, या no clear time?  
3. **Sequence check करें।** क्या एक past action दूसरे से पहले हुआ?  
4. **Helping verbs check करें।** **Did** के बाद base verb; **has/have/had** के बाद past participle.  
5. **Clause pattern check करें।** Future main clauses के बाद time/condition clauses often present simple लेते हैं।  
6. **Meaning के लिए पढ़ें।** Final tense timeline clear करे।

### Sample 1

> I did not met him.

**Did not** के बाद base verb आता है।

> I did not **meet** him.

### Sample 2

> She has gone yesterday.

**Yesterday** finished past time है।

> She **went** yesterday.

### Sample 3

> By the time I arrived, he left.

He left before I arrived. Earlier past action को past perfect चाहिए।

> By the time I arrived, he **had left**.

## Examples That Show the Difference

1. I did not **meet** him.  
2. She **went** yesterday.  
3. They **visited** Delhi last year.  
4. I **have finished** my homework.  
5. I **finished** my homework last night.  
6. By the time we reached, the train **had left**.  
7. After she **had completed** the form, she submitted it.  
8. I will call you when I **reach** home.  
9. If it **rains** tomorrow, we will cancel the match.  
10. He **has lived** here for ten years.

Same verb different clues के हिसाब से different tenses ले सकता है: **finished last night** simple past चाहता है; **completed now** present perfect ले सकता है।

## Exam Traps and Usage Notes

### Trap 1: Did के बाद Double Past

> Wrong: Did you **saw** him?  
> Right: Did you **see** him?

**Did** के बाद कभी past form use न करें।

### Trap 2: Yesterday के साथ Present Perfect

> Wrong: I have submitted the assignment yesterday.  
> Right: I **submitted** the assignment yesterday.

Finished past time sentence को simple past की ओर ले जाता है।

### Trap 3: Earlier Past Ignore करना

> Wrong: The movie started before we reached.  
> Better: The movie **had started** before we reached.

जब sentence clearly two past actions compare करता है, past perfect order explicit करता है।

### Trap 4: Time Clauses में Will

> Wrong: I will wait until he will come.  
> Right: I will wait until he **comes**.

Time clause में present simple use करें।

### Trap 5: Since and For

**Since** starting point दिखाता है; **for** duration दिखाता है। ये commonly present perfect या present perfect continuous के साथ आते हैं।

> She **has worked** here since 2020.  
> She **has worked** here for four years.

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Right | Why |
|---|---|---|
| I did not **met** him. | I did not **meet** him. | **Did** के बाद base verb. |
| She **has gone** yesterday. | She **went** yesterday. | Finished past time simple past लेता है। |
| By the time I arrived, he **left**. | By the time I arrived, he **had left**. | Earlier past action. |
| I will call when I **will reach**. | I will call when I **reach**. | Time clause present simple लेता है। |
| Did she **completed** the task? | Did she **complete** the task? | **Did** base verb लेता है। |

## Quick Practice

Correct option चुनें या error correct करें।

1. I did not ___ the answer.  
   a) knew b) know
2. She ___ yesterday.  
   a) has arrived b) arrived
3. By the time we came, they ___.  
   a) had eaten b) ate
4. I will inform you when he ___.  
   a) comes b) will come
5. Error spotting: Did you watched the match?
6. Error spotting: He has finished the work last night.
7. Error spotting: Before the police arrived, the thief escaped.
8. Fill in the blank: I ___ this book twice. (read / have read)
9. Rewrite correctly: I will leave after he will return.
10. Choose: She has lived here ___ 2018. (since / for)

### Answer Key

1. **know** — **did not** के बाद base verb.  
2. **arrived** — **yesterday** finished past time है।  
3. **had eaten** — eating हमारे आने से पहले हुआ।  
4. **comes** — future time clause में present simple.  
5. **Did you watch the match?**  
6. **He finished the work last night.**  
7. **Before the police arrived, the thief had escaped.**  
8. **have read** — life experience up to now.  
9. **I will leave after he returns.**  
10. **since** — starting point.

## Summary

> **Final rule:** Tense को time clue, action sequence और clause pattern से match करें। सिर्फ sound के आधार पर tense न चुनें।

Memory trick: **Time word first, tense choice second.**

ये revise करें:

- I did not **meet** him.  
- She **went** yesterday.  
- By the time I arrived, he **had left**.  
- I will call when I **reach**.
